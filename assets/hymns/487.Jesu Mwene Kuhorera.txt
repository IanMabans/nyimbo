Jesũ, Mwene Kũhorera(23)
(Gentle Jesus, meek and mild)

1.Jesũ, Mwene kũhorera,
  Rora mwana ũyũ mũkĩa;
  Ndũkorie mehia makwa;
  Reke njũke harĩ we.

2.Jesũ, Gatũrũme ka Ngai,
  We nĩwe njũthĩrĩirie;
  Ũndeithagie tondũ we
  Warĩ mwana ta niĩ.

3.Nyendaga kũhana tawe,
  O na niĩ njathĩkage:
  Jesũ, wĩ mũthingu ma,
  Na niĩ ngwenda gũthinga.

4.Jesũ Kristũ, mũhoreri,
  Ndĩ o waku we wiki:
  Njoya, ndũre harĩ we;
  Ngoro yakwa ndakũhe.

5.Mwathani, ndĩĩkũgathaga
  Na ngũiguage ma;
  Andũ nao manjĩtage,
  “Mwana ũyũ wa Jesũ.”

                 Joh 13:15
