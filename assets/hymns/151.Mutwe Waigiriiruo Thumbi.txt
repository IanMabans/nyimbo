Mũtwe Waigĩrĩirũo Thũmbĩ
(The head that once was crowned)

1.Mũtwe waigĩrĩirũo thũmbĩ.
  Ya mĩigua o tene,
  Rĩu ũgemetio na riri,
  Nĩ ũtoria wa Jesũ.

2.Gwake nĩ Igũrũ mũno,
  Mũnene wa anene;
  Mũthamaki wa athamaki,
  Na ũtheri wakuo.

3.Gĩkeno kĩa arĩa me Igũrũ,
  Na kĩa arĩa marĩ thĩ,
  Na arĩa onagia wendo,
  Na akamamenyithia.

4.Nĩmahetwo mũtharaba,
  Wĩ thoni na ũtugi;
  Rĩĩtwa rĩega rĩa gũtũra;
  Gĩkeno igũrũ.

5.Manyamario na Jesũ thĩ,
  Mathanaga Igũrũ;
  Makenaga maguũrĩrio
  Wendo wa Mwathani.

             Ahib 2:9
