Indo Ciaku Nocio(202)
(We give thee But Thine Own)

1.Indo ciaku nocio
  Tũgũcokagĩria;
  Amu iria tũrĩ nacio
  Tũrĩ aramati.

2.Reke icio ciothe
  Tũciamũkagĩre,
  Na mĩgerio yacio yothe
  Tũgakũnengera.

3.Ngoro nĩ ndihangie,
  Mĩciĩ ĩna ihoru;
  Na ng’ondu iria wakuĩrĩire
  Nĩihurunjũkĩte.

4.Rekithia mĩgwate;
  Nao arĩa morĩte
  Magũcokerere, Mwathi,
  Ta ũrĩa we wendaga.

5.Nĩtwĩtĩkĩrĩte
  Kiugo gĩaku kĩa ma:
  Tweka ciĩko ciitũ cia tha.
  Nĩwe twĩkagĩra.


           1 Kor 4:7
