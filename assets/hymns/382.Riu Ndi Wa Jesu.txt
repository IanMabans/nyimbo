Rĩu Ndĩ Wa Jesũ(295)
(Blessed Assuarance Jesũs is mine)

1.Rĩu ndĩ wa Jesũ, wake o ma!
  Nake nĩ wakwa, wakwa kũna!
  Ndĩ njiriiri ngoroinĩ;
  Rĩu tũrĩ ũmwe, Jesũ na niĩ.

         Ũhoro mwega rwĩmbo rwakwa-
         Ngoocage Jesũ hĩndĩ ciothe;
         Ndiũĩ ũndũ ũngĩ; njũĩ ũguo-
         Ngoocaga Jesũ hĩndĩ ciothe!

2.Ngai nĩangũũrire, Ngai mwene,
  Akĩndutĩra Mwana wake;
  Nĩ’honokete kũrĩ gĩkuũ
  Nĩ ũndũ wa Jesũ, muoyo witũ.

3.Roho Mũtheru, ngoro thĩinĩ
  Nĩanjiguithagia ũhoreri;
  We nĩanjĩraga ndikamake:
  Rĩu ndĩ wa Jesũ nginya tene.

4.Rĩu nĩndĩkĩtie gwĩtĩkĩra
  O ũrĩa Jesũ angĩnjĩra:
  Enda kũndũma, akĩndũme;
  Enda kũnjĩta, ‘thiĩ gwake.

                Thab 34:1-8
