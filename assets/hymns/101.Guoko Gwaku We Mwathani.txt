Guoko gwaku, We Mwathani(80)
(Thine Arm O Lord,in the Days of Old)

1.Guoko gwaku,we Mwathani,
  Kwarĩ na hinya ma,
  Wa gũtoria ndũari ciothe,
  Nduma o na gĩkuũ;
  Arũaru othe mokaga
  Makahonio nĩwe-
  Me na mangũ, na atumumu,
  Cionje na ithua.

2.Guoko gwaku, Kwarehage
  Ũgima na muoyo,
  Wahingũraga andũ maitho
  Na matũ ma itaigua;
  O ũguo taguo, Mwathani,
  Andũ nĩmabatiĩ:
  Mahutio na guoko gwaku
  Nĩguo mahonio biũ.

3.Jesũ Mũtoria wa gĩkuũ,
  Tũhatũrage ithuĩ,
  Tũrurumũkie,tũhonie
  Na mĩhũmũ yaku;
  Nawe Ngai ũrogathwo
  Nĩ agima na cionje,
  We Mwene-tha na Mwendani
  Ũrokumio guothe!

             Mat 11:5
