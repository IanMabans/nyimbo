Atatũ Thĩinĩ wa ũmwe
(“Three in One, and One in Three”)

1.Atatũ thĩinĩ wa ũmwe,
  Ũmwe thĩinĩ wa atatũ
  Mwathani wa andũ othe,
  Twakũgooca We.

2.ũtheri wa motheri
  Ũtũtherie ithuĩ ;
  Nĩguo wendo ũtonye
  Ngoroinĩ rĩu.

3.ũtheri wa motheri
  Tũgĩkinya hwaĩinĩ,
  Tũkwenda tũgakorũo
  Tũrĩ na thayũ.

4.Atatũ thĩinĩ wa ũmwe,
  Ũmwe thĩinĩ wa Atatũ;
  Tũgũkumagie thĩ ĩno,
  O na Igũrũ.

            Mat 3:19
