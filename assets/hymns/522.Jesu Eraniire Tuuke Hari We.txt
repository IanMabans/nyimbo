Jesũ Eranĩire Tũũke Harĩ We
(We are but little Children weak)

1.Jesũ eranĩire tũũre harĩ We,
  O na tũrĩ twana tũnini mũno;

              Tũngĩmwĩtĩkia rĩu tũrĩ twana,
              Twatuĩka tũrata twake
              Nĩguo oigire.

2.Tũrata twa Jesũ, tũkenaga ma,
  Tondũ ciugo ciake itwĩraga ũũ;

3.Tũrata twa Jesũ, tũceere nake,
  Nĩguo tũnyitane na moko nake:

4.Ni tũmwende mũno na ngoro ciitũ,
  Na nĩtũkamuona ũthiũ na ũthiũ.

                   Ayub 20:11
