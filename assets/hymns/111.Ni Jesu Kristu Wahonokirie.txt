Nĩ Jesũ Kristũ Wa’honokirie
(Jesus has Loved Me-Wonderful Saviour)

1.Nĩ Jesũ Kristũ wa’honokirie,
  Nĩanguĩrĩire,o na ndĩ mwĩhia.
  Tondũ wa ũguo nĩndĩmwendete.
  No we Mũnene wa ngoro yakwa.

        Kumiai Jesũ,Mũkũũri wakwa,
        Mũkumie amu nowe ndĩaga.
        Kumiai Jesũ,Mũkũũri wakwa,
        Mũmũkumiei nginya o tene.

2.Nĩ Jesũ Kristũ ũnyendete ma,
  Wendo wake ndũngĩheanĩka.
  Nĩokire thĩ,akũũre ahia
  Arĩa matũire ũkomboinĩ.

          Tito 2:14
