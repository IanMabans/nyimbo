Kũraya Kũraya
(Far, Far Away Beyond the river)

1.Kũraya, kũraya, matuinĩ,
  Mbere itũ:
  Gĩkeno gĩa tene na tene,
  Tũkona matuinĩ.

           Kĩeha, ruo, gũtigana,
           Kũu gũtirĩ,
           Thuthainĩ twaringa mũrĩmo,
           Mũciĩ wa matuinĩ.

2.Kũraya, kũraya, arata
  Nĩmathire:
  Tũkamakora kũu Matuinĩ;
  Tũtũre nao kuo.

3.Kũraya, kũraya, kwa Baba,
  Ngoro yakwa;
  Nĩngwenda gũthiĩ kuo ngamuone;
  Tũtũranie nake.

               Kũg 21:1
