Mbũri Cianathĩnjwo(102)
(Not All the Blood of Beast)

1.Mbũri cianathĩnjwo
  Cia ngoma na cia Ngai;
  Cianaaga gũtũtheria
  Na gũtũhe muoyo.

2.Jesũ nĩokire thĩ,
  Agĩkua mũtĩinĩ;
  No thakame yake iiki
  Ĩngĩtheria ngoro.

3.Thiĩrĩ wakwa wothe
  Wa waganu wakwa
  Nĩũrĩhĩtwo nĩ Kristũ
  Na thakame yake.

4.Ngai arogoocwo,
  Ithe,Mũriũ na Roho,
  Nĩ ũndũ wa ũhonokio
  Ũcio twaheirũo.

           Ahib 10:4
