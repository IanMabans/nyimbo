We, Roho Mũtheru(143)
(O Holy Spirit)

1.We, Roho Mũtheru,
  Ũka gũkũ gwitũ
  Na ũtuonie ũũru
  Ũrĩ ngoro ciitũ.

2.Mbere twacangaga
  Kũraya na gwaku;
  Rĩu tũgũkũhoya
  Wohanĩri waku.

3.We, Roho Mũtheru,
  Tũũra kũndũ gũkũ;
  Nina nduma iitũ
  Na ũtheri waku.

4.Ningĩ wakie mwaki
  Ngoroinĩ ciothe
  Kinya icinĩrũo
  Wĩhia wacio wothe.

5.We, Roho Mũtheru
  Ikaraga gwitũ;
  Tũrutagwo nĩwe
  O mĩthenya yothe.

6.Tũtirĩ na hinya
  Gwĩthondeka ngoro;
  Tuonie Jesũ Kristũ,
  Mũhonokia witũ.

7.We, Roho Mũtheru,
  Ũka gũkũ gwitũ
  Nĩtũgwetereire-
  Hiũha, we Mũtheru!


             Joh 16:13-14
