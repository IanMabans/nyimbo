60 THAKAME NDATHIME NĨYO

(BLESSED BE THE FOUNTAIN OF BLOOD)

1.	Thakame ndathime nĩyo
Ĩtathambagia ithuĩ.
Mũrũ wa mũmwe wa Ngai,
Nĩwe watũkuĩrĩire.
Njagĩrĩirũo no gũcirithio,
Na ndingĩhota kwĩhonokia;
Nĩngwenda thakame ĩo
Ĩ'therie ndigacirithio.
Mũtheru mũno, mũtheru mũno,
Ndathambio thakameinĩ,
No ngĩtuĩke mũtheru ma.

2.	Tene Jesũ nĩakuire,
Agĩkua handũ hakwa;
Na nĩathĩnirio mũno,
Agĩthĩna nĩ kũnyenda.
Nĩngwenda gwĩthamba rĩu
Na thakame yake iiki,
Ngoro yakwa yarĩ gĩko
Ĩtuĩke theru mũno.

3.	Baba, nĩnjĩhĩtie mũno,
Ngoro yakwa nĩ njũru ma:
Ndĩ na mehia maingĩ ũguo,
Mũhonokia ngaruta ha?
Jesũ nĩngũka o rĩu
Rũũĩiniĩ rũu rwa thakame,
Nĩruo rũninaga mehia,
'Thambia ngoro 'there ta ira.
