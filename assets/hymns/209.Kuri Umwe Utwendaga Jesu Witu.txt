Kũrĩ ũmwe ũtwendaga-Jesũ Witũ(163)
(One there is Above all others)

1.Kũrĩ ũmwe ũtwendaga-Jesũ witũ;
  Ũguo noguo tũmwĩtaga,Jesũ witũ
  Tũtiũĩ Kwenda mũndũ,
  O na tũngĩgĩa ndũgũ;
  Jesũ nowe mwendi andũ,Jesũ witũ.

2.Wee nĩũkwenda kũmũmenya,Jesũ witũ?
  Ũngĩmwĩta nĩegũkũigua,Jesũ witũ:
  Rĩu e hakuhĩ nawe
  Akĩenda atĩ mũmenyane
  Na mũtume ndũgũ nake,Jesũ witũ.

3.We nowe Mũrata waku,Jesũ witũ:
  Mwende ma na ngoro yaku,Jesũ witũ.
  Nde hingo agagũtiga:
  O na andũ maagũthũũra,
  Nĩũgũtũũra na Mũrata,Jesũ witũ.

4.Wehia we ndagagũkũma,Jesũ witũ;
  We no tha agakũiguĩra,Jesũ witũ;
  Agakũhe hinya wake
  Ũhote gũthinga take
  Nĩguo ũkaambata gwake,Jesũ witũ.

                       Joh 13:34
