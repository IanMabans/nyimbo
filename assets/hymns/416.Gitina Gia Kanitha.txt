Gĩtina Gĩa Kanitha(278)
(The church’s one foundation)

1.Gĩtina Gĩa Kanitha Nĩ Mwathani witũ,
  Nake nĩwe wokire Akiuma igũrũ,
  Oke gũkũ acarie Andũ arĩa morĩte,
  Nĩgetha amagũre Na thakame yake.

2.Kanitha ũcio no ũmwe Wa kĩrĩndĩ gĩothe
  Gĩa thĩ na kĩa igũrũ, Na njĩra no ĩmwe.
  Irio ciaguo no imwe, Wĩtĩkio no ũmwe,
  Na andũ aguo othe Magoocaga Kristũ.

3.Kanitha nĩũmenagwo Ũgathĩnio mũno,
  Na ningĩ ũgathũkio Na nĩ amwe arĩa aguo;
  No othe arĩa atheru Mahoyaga Kristũ,
  Ũtukũ ũyũ ũthire, Gũthere kĩroko.

4.Ongorũo nĩũthĩnagio Na mbaara wĩ nayo,
  Kanitha nĩũkenaga, Ningĩ wĩ na thayũ.
  Hatigairie hanini Thĩna ũgathira
  Atheru mahootana Makahurũkio ma.

5.Ithuothe a gũkũ thĩ Nĩtũagĩrĩirũo
  Tũgwatane na Mwathi Na arĩa me igũrũ:
  Nĩguo o na ithuĩ Tũkinye kwa Ngai
  Hamwe na acio tũtuĩke Mũhiki wa Mwathi.

                      1 Kor 3:11
