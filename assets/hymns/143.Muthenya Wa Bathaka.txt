Mũthenya Wa Bathaka
(This joyful Eastertide)

1.Mũthenya wa Bathaka,
  Nĩ thigũkũ kenai;
  Jesũ warĩ mwambe,
  Nĩ ariũkire kĩroko.

          ‘Korũo Jesũ ndariũkire,
          Mũthenya wa itatũ,
          No twage mwĩhoko
          No rĩu nĩ ariũkĩte.

2.Muoyo wakwa wothe
  Ndĩmwĩhokete Kristũ;
  Akuũ makĩriũkio
  Ngatũra muoyo nake.

3.Gĩkuũ nĩ gĩtorie,
  Jesũ aringa rũũĩ
  Mwathani mwega ma,
  ‘Honokia ngoka gwaku.

       Luk 24:6
