Jesus, Mũkũũri Witũ(325)
(Jesus from thy throne on high)

1.Jesũ, Mũkũũri witũ,
  Twagũcokeria ngatho,
  Wee ũtũmenyereire
  Mwaka ũcio wathira.

2.Wee igongona riitũ,
  Twarehe mehia maitũ
  Tũmahumbũrĩtie
  Nginya gĩtĩinĩ gĩaku,

3.Rĩrĩa twagĩte hinya
  Wee wĩ rwaro rwa ihiga;
  Thĩinĩ wa mĩoyo iitũ
  Tuonagie njĩra ya ma.

4.O mũndũ ũrĩa ũgeta
  Mwaka ũyũ tũratonya,
  Tuĩka mwĩgito wake
  Ũmũninĩre guoya.

5.ũtũtue ehokeku
  Tũtũũrage twĩ aku:
  Tũngĩyũmagĩrĩria
  Nĩtũkaheo thũmbĩ.

         Thab 65:11-13
