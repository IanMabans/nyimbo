ũkĩrai Narua , Thigari Cia Kristũ(237)
(Soldiers of Christ Arise)

1.ũkĩrai narua, thigari cia Kristũ,
  Mwĩohe indo cia ita, o iria cia Ngai.

2.ũrĩa wĩhokaga o hinya wa Jesũ
  Ũcio nĩarĩhootanaga mbaarainĩ ciothe.

3.Mwĩhande na hinya mũnene wa Ngai,
  Mwĩhotorete indo iria iturĩirũo gwake.

4.Mwĩgitĩre guothe, thũ ndĩkone mweke;
  Twaranagai Hĩndĩ ciothe na Mũmũtongoria.

5.Rĩrĩa tũkũhoya nĩrĩo tũrarwa,
  Nĩguo mbũtũ cia Caitani igakĩmemendwo.

6.Ngoroinĩ cianyu Roho athaithanage,
  “ũka, Jesũ, ũikĩinũkie athigari aku!”

                   Ef 6:10-18
