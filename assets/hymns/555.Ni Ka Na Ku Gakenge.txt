Nĩ Ka Na Kũ Gakenge(91)
(Who is He in the Yonder stall)

1.Nĩ ka na kũ gakenge
  Kaige nyũmba ya ng’ombe?

       Ũcio nĩwe Mũhonokia,
       Nĩwe Mũthamaki witũ!
       Nĩtũgũe thĩ harĩ we,
       Tũmũtue Mũnene.

2.Mũtharamara ũrĩa nũ
  Ũrateithania na Ithe?

3.ũrĩa mũgerie mũno
  E mũhũtũ, nake nũ?

4.Nũ ũyũ ũkũrĩra
  Mũrata wake aakua?

5.Mũgũnda ũtukũ
  Ũrĩa wĩ na kĩeha, nũ?

6.ũyũ nũ ũrathĩnio
  Mwambe mũtĩ Igũrũ?

7.Na ũrĩa wariũka, nũ,
  Atoretie gĩkuũ?

8.Rĩu Mũnene nĩ ũrĩkũ
  Wathanaga Igũrũ?

              Filip 2:9-11
