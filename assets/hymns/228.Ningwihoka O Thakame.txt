Nĩngwĩhoka O Thakame(179)
(My Hope is Built on Nothing Less)

1.Nĩngwĩhoka thakame
  Na ũthingu wa Mwathani;
  Ndingĩĩhoka rĩĩtwa rĩngĩ,
  No rĩa Mũhonokia witũ.

          Nĩndĩhandĩte ihigainĩ,
          Kũndũ kũingĩ kwĩ ũgwati;
          Kũndũ kũngĩ kwĩ ũgwatĩ;

2.Mahinda ma nduma nene
  Ndĩhokaga ngoga yake:
  Rĩrĩa ngucagio nĩ ũũru
  ‘Hoyaga Jesũ andeithie.

3.Nĩndeithagio nĩ mwĩhĩtwa
  Na kĩrĩkanĩro gĩake;
  Nĩ’theragio mehia mothe
  Tondũ wa thakame yake.

4.Jesũ nĩagoka gũkũ thĩ,
  Aiyĩre arata ake,
  Akĩmatware Igũrũ,
  Maikare Igũrũ,
  Maikare mũciĩ wake.

             Thab 40:1-3
