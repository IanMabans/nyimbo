Ngũtherio Ngoro Nĩ Kĩ?(176)
(What can wash away my stain?)

1.Ngũtherio ngoro nĩ kĩ?
  Nĩ thakame yaku, Jesũ.
  Ngũhonokio nĩ ndũũ?
  Nĩ thakame yaku,Jesũ.

      Ĩĩ, Jesũ Kristũ!
      Thakame yaku noyo
      Ĩgũ’thambia ngoro,
      No thakame yaku,Jesũ.

2.Kĩruti kĩa maũũru
  No thakame yaku,Jesũ,
  Ti mũthaiga wĩ hinya;
  No thakame yaku,Jesũ.

3.Kĩrĩhi kĩa mathirĩ
  Nĩ thakame yaku, Jesũ.
  Ti ta mbia na mbũri;
  Nĩ thakame yaku,iiki.

4.Nĩngahaica kwa Ngai
  Na thakame yaku iiki;
  Ndonye kũu Igũrũ
  Na thakame yaku iiki.

                  Ahib 10:19,20
