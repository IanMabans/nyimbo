Ngwenda Kũmũmenya Jesũ.
(More About Jesus Would I Know)

1.Ngwenda Kumũmenya Jesũ,
  Menyithanie wega wake;
  Nyonanie ũhonokio wake,
  Na wendani wa Mũkũũri.

       Kũmũmenya Jesũ
       O mũno makĩria,
       Wendo mũingĩ na ũhonokio;
       Jesũ ũrĩa wanguĩrĩire.

2.Ngwenda Kũmũmenya Jesũ,
  Na menye ũrĩa endaga;
  Roho wake amenyithie,
  Maũndũ ma Jesũ Kristũ.

3.Kũmenya kiugo kĩa Jesũ,
  Kũgĩa ngwatanĩro nake;
  Na kũigua mũgambo wake,
  Nyongagĩrĩrwo wĩtĩkio.

4.Kũmenya ũnene wa Jesũ,
  Ũtonga wa riri wake;
  Mũthamaki wa Athamaki,
  No we Mũnene wa thayũ.


          Filip 3:10
