Mwathani Jesũ Nĩaretana(192)

1.Mwathani Jesũ nĩaretana,
  Tiganĩria mĩago ya thĩ ĩno,
  Thĩ ĩno ndĩrĩ na ũhurũko;
  Ũkĩra toroinĩ waku.
         Nĩaragwĩta,
         Hiũha narua,
         Ndũgeterere,
         Ũka rĩu kwĩ jesũ.

2.Mwathani Jesũ nĩaretana,
  Tiga gũtuuria ngoro yake,
  Ũikaire wetereire kĩ?
  Ũka rĩu, nĩaragwĩta.

3.Mwathi Jesũ nĩaretana,
  Watũũra ũkomboinĩ nĩkĩ?
  Ũnyariragwo nĩ mehia?
  Jesũ nĩegũkũhurũkia.

4.Mwathi Jesũ nĩaranjĩta,
  Ngoro yakwa ndamũhe yothe,
  Thĩ nĩndamiugĩra ũhoro
  Tondũ Jesũ nĩanjiganĩte.

          Luk 9:57-62
