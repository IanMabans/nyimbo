Nĩtwĩhokage Jesũ(232)
(Simply Trusting Every Day)

1.Nĩtwĩhokage Jesũ,
  Rĩrĩa njĩra ĩĩ njũru;
  Thakame yake nĩyo,
  Igatũkinyia kwĩ muoyo.

         Nĩtwĩhokage Jesũ
         Ngoroinĩ ti itherũ,
         O nginya tũgakinya
         Handũ hake hega ma.

2.Roho Mũtheru wake,
  Ũka ũtũmũrĩkĩre;
  Ũtheri waku nĩguo
  Ũtũgiragia tũhĩngwo.

3.Nĩ kũina tũrĩinaga
  Rĩrĩa twĩ rũgendoinĩ;
  Twatwarũo nĩ ũtheri ũcio,
  Tũtikahĩtia njĩra.

4.Twĩhokage thaa ciothe,
  O na mĩthenya yothe;
  Ningĩ rĩrĩa tũgakua,
  Nĩtũgakinya Igũrũ.

             Ef 1:7-9, 13, 14
