Mwathani Witũ, Tũrathime(331)
(Lord dismiss us with thy Blessings)

1. Mwathani witũ tũrathime
  Tũhe gĩkeno na thayũ;
  Ũtũiyũrie wega waku
  Na wendani tũtorie:
  Tũnogore tũkĩinũka
  Rũgendo-inĩ rũa thĩ ĩno.

2.Twakũgatha tũkenete
  Nĩ ũhoro waku mwega,
  Maciaro na ũhonokio
  Marotũiyũra ngoro:
  Ũrotũũranagia na ithuĩ
  Nginya mĩndĩ na mĩndĩ.

3.Rĩrĩa tũgetwo tuume thĩ
  Tũiyũkio nĩ araika,
  Tũgetĩka tũkenete,
  Coro waku wahuhwo:
  Tũma tũgathamakaga
  Na Kristũ nginya tene.

       1 Ath 19:5-8
