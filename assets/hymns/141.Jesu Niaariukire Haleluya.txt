Jesũ Nĩaariũkire,Haleluya(115)
(Jesus Christ has Risen Today)

1.Jesũ nĩaariũkire,Haleluya!!
  Akiuma kũrĩ akuũ,Haleluya!!
  Nĩambirũo mũtĩinĩ,Haleluya!!
  Agĩtuĩka Mũkũũri,Haleluya!!

2.Tũgooce Mwathani witũ,Haleluya!!
  Tondũ niwe Mũkũũri,Haleluya!!
  Nĩokire gũkũ thĩ,Haleluya!!
  Atũhonokagie,Haleluya!!

3.Thakame nĩyaitirũo,Haleluya!!
  Ya gũtheria ngoro ciitũ:Haleluya!!
  Rĩu atũire Igũrũ;Haleluya!!
  Nĩagacoka gũkũ rĩngĩ,Haleluya!!

4.Ngai Ithe arogoocwo,Haleluya!!
  Na Jesũ Mũkũũri witũ,Haleluya!!
  O na Roho Mũtheru,Haleluya!!
  Ũtatũ wa Ngai ũmwe,Haleluya!!

                  Mat 28:6
