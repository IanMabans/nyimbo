Jesũ Mũkũũri Witũ, We ũtũire(123)
(Jesus from thy throne on high)

1.Jesũ Mũkũũri witũ,
  We ũtũire Igũrũ,
  Tũririkane rĩu,
  Ũtũigue, Jesũ.

2.Watuika wĩ hakuhĩ,
  Tũtikona ũgwati.
  Wendaga andũ othe,
  Ũtũigue, Jesũ.

3.Tũgũkũhoya rĩu
  Utũthambie ngoro
  Thakameinĩ yaku,
  Ũtũigue, Jesũ.

4.Utũhe ithuothe
  Roho waku Mũtheru,
  Atũtherie mũno,
  Ũtũigue, Jesũ.

5.Iromo ũgirie
  Itikoige ũũru,
  Itigakũrakarie,
  Ũtũigue,Jesũ.

6.Mahinda maitũ mothe
  Tũtuĩke atheru ma,
  Tũtigane na ũũru,
  Ũtũigue, Jesũ.

7.Tũtuĩke andũ a ma,
  Twende gũthikĩrĩria
  Ndeto ciaku haha rĩu,
  Ũtũigue, Jesũ.

8.Jesũ nĩwe wokire
  Nĩguo ũtũhonokie:
  Ũtũteithie rĩu,
  Ũtũigue, Jesũ.


       Mat 18:19, 20
