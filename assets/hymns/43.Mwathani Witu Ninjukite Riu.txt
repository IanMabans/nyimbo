Mwathani witũ Nĩnjũkĩte Rĩu(35)
(Lord jesus I long to be perfectly whole)

1.Mwathani witũ nĩnjũkĩte rĩu;
  Nĩngwenda ũnjiyũrie thinya waku;
  Ũndeithie Mwathani ũnjiguĩre tha:
  Nĩngwenda ũtherie ngoro yakwa.
        Ndeithia o rĩu Mwathani witũ,
        Ũ’he hinya waku na wendo waku.

2.Roho Mũtheru,nĩwe ũngĩhota
  Kũnjiyũria hinya, na wendo na tha;
  Njiguĩre andũ matarĩ aku kĩeha,
  Na kĩyo kĩnene kĩa wĩra waku.

3.Awa Mũtheru, 0 rĩu nĩndakũhe
  Ngoro yakwa,nduĩke mũtheru tawe;
  Ũ’he ngoro njerũ ta mũnyũ mwerũ,
  ‘Hianage ta Jesũ Mwathani witũ.

                   Thab 51:1-7
