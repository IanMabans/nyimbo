Andũ A Mũthamaki(216)
(Children of the Heavenly King)

1.Andũ a Mũthamaki,
  Inagai mũgĩthiĩ;
  Ganagai maũndũ
  Ma Mũhonokia wanyu!

2.Nĩ kwa Ngai tũgũthiĩ,
  Tũinũke kũu Igũrũ;
  Tũgakenanĩre kuo
  Na arĩa maanahonokio.

3.Mũtikĩamũkĩrie-ni,
  Kĩrĩndĩ kĩa Mwathani!
  Mũgatũũra na Jesũ
  Bũrũri wa gĩkeno.

4.Rĩu tirai maitho:
  Mũciĩ witũ nĩũkuonwo:
  Nĩkuo tũkahurũka,
  Kũu kwa Mũhonokia.

5.Tigagai kũnoga;
  Kinyagai na hinya :
  Jesũ mwene nĩoigĩte
  Ndagatũtiga o rĩ.

6.Mwathani, tũtongorie:
  Tũkwenda ũtũtware:
  Indo ciitũ twakũhe
  Tũkũrũmĩrĩire we.

           Isa 35:8-10
