ũgooci O Na Riri(95)
(All glory Laud and Honour)

     Ũgooci o na riri
     Nĩ ciaku Mũkũũri,
     Nĩwe ũrĩa wainĩirũo
     Nĩ ciana,”Hosana”.

1.Nĩwe Mũrũ wa Daudi,
  Mũthamaki witũ;
  Ũgũũka wĩ mũrathime
  Na rĩĩtwa rĩa Mwathi.

2.Araika mbũtũ nene
  Nĩmaragũkumia,
  Makaamũkanĩria hamwe
  Na andũ o na ciũmbe.

3.Aisiraeli a tene
  Magĩgũthiĩ mbere,
  Manyitĩte makĩndũ
  Na magakũgana.

4.ũrĩa maakũgoocire
  Ũtanathĩnio ma,
  Ũguo noguo twagũkumia
  Mwathi mũtũgĩru.

5.Wakeneire ngemi icio;
  Ũtũigue o na ithuĩ,
  We Mũthamaki mũtugi,
  Tũgĩgũkũngũĩra.

           Mat 21:1-16
