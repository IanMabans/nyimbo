Jesũ,Riũa Rĩa Wega(51)
(jesus sun of righteousness)

1.Jesũ, Riũa rĩa wega,
  Ara ngoroinĩ ciitũ;
  Twĩonithie,twagũthaitha;
  Ruta nduma yothe iitũ.

2.Ithuĩ nĩtũthiaga
  Tũhana ta atumumu:
  Riũa rĩa wega,ũka,
  Ũgatuonie njĩra iitũ.

3.Ngoroinĩ ũtarĩ ho
  No nduma ndumanu  harĩ:
  Riũa riitũ, woneke,
  Nĩgguo tũgĩe na ũtheri.
4.Mũrĩkĩra ngoro ciitũ,
  Nduma yothe ĩgĩthire;
  Gũtirĩ gatuma gwaku,
  No ũtheri hingo ciothe.

              Joh 12:35,36,44-46
