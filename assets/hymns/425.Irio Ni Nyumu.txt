Irio Nĩ Nyũmũ
(The fields are all white)

1.Irio nĩ nyũmũ;
  Agethi me-kũ?
  Ithuĩ twĩ twana;
  Tũhote ũndũ
  Tũteithie Mwathani kũgetha?

2.Twakĩaga hinya
  Na twaaga ũũgĩ,
  Nĩ atĩa tũgwĩka
  Nĩguo tũhote
  Gũteithia Mwathani kũgetha?

3.Nĩ ‘riri, twĩke,
  Tũhoyage Ngai
  Na twĩrute wega
  Ũhoro wake,
  Tũteithie Mwathani kũgetha?

4.Na twaikara,
  No tũgakũra
  Tũtuĩke agethi
  A hinya mũno,
  Tuteithie Mwathani Kũgetha?

                  Joh 4:35
