Thikĩrĩria ũhoro Mwega
(O Listen to A wondrous story)

1.Thikĩrĩria ũhoro mwega
  Twatũire ndumainĩ;
  Jesũ agĩũka akiuma Igũrũ,
  Agĩtũgũra goro.

       Nũ watũhonokirie?
       Nĩ Jesũ Mũtharabainĩ,
       Ekire atĩa?Nĩakuire;
       Eha rĩu?ĩtĩkia We.
       E Igũrũ agĩtũhoera.

2.Gũtirĩ mũraika ũngĩakuire
  Ta ũguo Jesũ akuire;
  Akĩambwo na agĩthirĩkio:
  Ũmwe thĩinĩ wa ũtatũ.

3.Nĩũkwĩheana he Mwathani
  Rĩu ũmũinamĩrĩre?
  Na nĩũkũmenya wega wake
  Na nĩegũkũhonokia.

                Rom 5:6-9
