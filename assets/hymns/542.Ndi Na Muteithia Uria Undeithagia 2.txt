Ndĩ Na Mũteithia ũrĩa ũndeithagia
(I have a savior who’s Mighty to keep)

1.Ndĩna Mũteithia ũrĩa ũndeithagia,
  Ũndeithagia, ũndeithagia;
  Ndĩna Mũteithia ũrĩa ũndeithagia,
  Ũndeithagia kaingĩ ma.

2.Ndĩna Mũhei ũrĩa ũ’heaga,
  Ũ'heaga, ũ’heaga;
  Ndĩna Mũhei ũrĩa ũ’heaga,
  Ũ'heaga kaingĩ ma.

3.Ndĩna Mũndwari ũrĩa ũndwaraga,
  Ũndwaraga, ũndwaraga;
  Ndĩna Mũndwari ũrĩa ũndwaraga,
  Ũndwaraga kaingĩ ma.
