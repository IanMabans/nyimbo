Mũrũ wa Ngai Nĩokire(105)
(Man of sorrows what a name)

1.Mũrũ wa Ngai Nĩokire
  Etagwo.”Wa mathĩĩna,”
  Nĩguo atũhonokie;
  Mũhonokia mwega mũno.

2.Nĩaacirithirio
  Ningĩ nĩaahũrirũo ma;
  Na nĩwe wanjoheire;
  Mũhonokia mwega mũno.

3.Ithuĩ twarĩ oru ma,
  No we aarĩ mũthingu;
  Nĩaatũiguithanirie ;
  Mũhonokia mwega mũno.

4.Nĩambirũo mũtĩinĩ
  Nĩoigire,”Nĩ’hingĩtie;”
  Rĩu atũire Igũrũ;
  Mũhonokia mwega mũno.

5.Rĩrĩa agoka,mũnene,
  Atũtware Igũrũ,
  Hĩndĩ ĩo nĩtũkaina,
  “Mũhonokia mwega mũno.”

                Isa 53:3-5
