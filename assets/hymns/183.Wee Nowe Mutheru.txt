Wee Nowe Mũtheru(8)
(Holy,Holy,Holy,Lord God Almighty)

1.Wee nowe Mũtheru,Ngai Mũrungu witũ;
  Rũcinĩ tene nĩtũgũkũinĩra :
  Wee nowe Mũtheru,mwega na mũthingu;
  Ngai nĩ ũtatũ,ningĩ no ũmwe.

2.Wee nowe Mũtheru ,araika atheru
  Nĩmegũkũgooca magĩgũkumagia,
  Harĩ Akerubi,o na Aserafi,
  Othe nĩmegũkũinamĩrĩra.

3.Wee nowe Mũtheru;ũrĩ hithoinĩ,
  Ndũngĩhota kuonwo nĩ maitho maitũ:
  Wee nowe Mwathani,gũtirĩ ũngĩ tawe:
  We mwene wendo;ũtũrathime.

4.Wee nowe Mũtheru, Ngai Mũrungu witũ;
  Rũcinĩ tene nĩtũgũkũinĩra;
  Wee nowe Mũtheru,Mwega na mũthingu;
  Ngai nĩ ũtatũ,ningĩ no ũmwe.


                  Isa 6:1-4
