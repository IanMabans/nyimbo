O Rũcinĩ Twarahũka(13)
(New every morning is the Love)

1.O rũcinĩ twarahũka
  Nĩtuonaga wendo waku;
  Nĩũtũraragia ũtukũ
  Kinya tũũkĩre na hinya.

2.Tha ciaku nĩcionanagia
  O mũthenya tũkĩhoya;
  Nĩtwoheirũo mehia maitũ
  Tũkagitĩrwo mogwati.

3.O mũthenya wĩtũ
  Nĩũtũrehagĩra mweke
  Wa gũtũruta kwĩrega
  Na gũkuhĩrĩria Ngai.

4.Rĩu Mwathani twakũũria
  Twĩtĩkĩrĩkage nĩwe;
  O mũthenya tũteithagie
  Tũtũre ta ũguo twahoya.

           Maca 3:22-23
