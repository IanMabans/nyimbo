Mũigue mũgambo wa Jesũ(86)
(where he leads I’ll follow)

1.Mũigue mũgambo wa Jesũ;
  Nĩ Mwathani witũ Jesũ,
  Nĩ Mũkũũri witũ jesũ;
  Egũtwĩta tũmũhikĩre.

          Nĩndĩkinyagĩra Jesũ;
          Kũrĩa guothe arĩndwaraga,
          Nĩndĩkinyagĩra Jesũ;
          E na ngoro yakwa yothe.

2.”Mũtiũkĩre njĩra yakwa,
  Njĩra yakwa nĩyo njega;
  Mũtituĩrie njĩra yakwa
  Na makinya makwa mothe!”

3.”Njĩra yakwa nĩyo njeke;
  O na ningĩ nĩ ngunderu,
  No nĩ njĩra ya kwambata,
  Ya gũkinyania Igũrũ.”

4.”Njĩra yakwa nĩ ya muoyo,
  Na nĩ njĩra ya gĩkeno,
  Gĩkeno gĩtagathira
  Mũciĩinĩ wakwa Igũrũ.”

                  Joh 21:15-19
