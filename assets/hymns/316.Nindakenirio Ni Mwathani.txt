*Nĩndakenirio Nĩ Mwathani(250)
(Redeemed How I love to proclaim it)

1.Nĩndakenirio nĩ Mwathani,
  Ũrĩa wanjiguĩrĩire tha,
  Thogora wa thakame yake
  Nĩ ũnduĩte mwana wake.

        Nĩngũũrĩtwo, nĩngũũrĩtwo,(gũkũũrũo)
        Nĩngũũrĩtwo na thakame (ya Jesũ),
        Nĩngũũrĩtwo, nĩngũũrĩtwo,
        Nĩngũũrĩtwo na thakame.

2.Nĩndakenirio nĩ gũkũũrũo,
  Gĩkeno gĩtangĩgwetwo;
  Na nĩkĩo kĩonanagia wendo,
  Ũrĩa wanduire wake ki!

3.Nĩngenagio nĩ wendo wake
  Mũnene wa kũgegania,
  Nĩngenagio nĩ watho wake.
  Ndĩinagĩra rĩĩtwa rĩake.

4.Nĩnjũũĩ nĩnjigĩirũo thũmbĩ,
  Ya muoyo kũrĩa Igũrũ
  Atigairie hanini acoke.
  Nĩguo harĩa arĩ nginye ho.

                 Kũg 1:5, 6
