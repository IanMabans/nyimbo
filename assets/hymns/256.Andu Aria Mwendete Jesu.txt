Andũ Arĩa Mwendete Jesũ (200)

1.Andũ arĩa mwendete Jesũ
  Hunjiai ũhonokio;
  Mũhunjĩrie andũ othe,
  Jesũ nĩ Mũhonokia.

                   Nĩngenete mũno ma,(Haleluya)
                   Nĩ gwĩtĩkia Mwathani,(We Jesũ)
                   Aakorire ndĩrigĩtwo,(Ndĩrigĩtwo)
                   Akĩnduta mehiainĩ.

2.Inyuĩ arĩa mũtũmĩtwo,
  Kwĩ ng’ondu iria ciũrĩte,
  Mũthiĩ mũkamere atĩrĩ,
  Ũhonokio nĩ mũũku.

3.Thakame ya Jesũ Kristũ
  Nĩĩtũtheragia,
  O na arĩa me mehiainĩ
  Mahonokagio nĩyo.

4.Nĩngenagio nĩ thakame,
  Nĩĩ’thambagia ngoro;
  Aakorire ndĩ mehiainĩ,
  Agĩ’theria ngoro ki.

            Luk 15:3-10
