Ndwara Kwĩ Jesũ, Ndwara Kwĩ Jesũ(352)
(Lead me to Jesus)

1.Ndwara kwĩ Jesũ, ndwara kwĩ Jesũ,
  Na ũndeithie ndĩmwende mũno:
  We wiki nowe Mũtũhonokia,
  Nĩngwenda ndĩmwĩtĩkie rĩu.

        Nĩtũthiĩ kũrĩ Jesũ na mĩtũkĩ,
        Nĩguo tũgathe Mũhonokia witũ.
        Tigai rĩu mĩhang’o ya thĩ,
        Nĩguo tũhoe Jesũ rĩu.

2.Ndwara kwĩ Jesũ, ndwara kwĩ Jesũ,
  Nĩwe mwega na mũhoreri.
  Nĩanjĩtaga, ndĩmũhikĩre
  Akĩenda kũnyamũkĩra.

3.Njĩra ũhoro, ũhoro wake,
  Atĩ nĩwe Maĩ ma Muoyo,
  Ũrĩa ũnyotiĩ nĩoke anyue,
  Anyotorũo ngoro nĩmo.

4.Nĩngũũka gwaku Mwathani Jesũ,
  Ũnjiguĩre tha, ũnjiyũkie:
  Ũ’thambie ngoro, na ũ’he Muoyo,
  Nĩ getha nduĩke tawe rĩu.

             Mar 2:1-12
