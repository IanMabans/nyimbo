Ikũrũkĩria Wendo Kuuma Igũrũ(307)
(Oh perfect Love, All Human Thought  Transcending)

1.Ikũrũkĩra wendo kuuma Igũrũ
  Aya maturĩtie ndu haha rĩu;
  Ndungata ciaku itũũranie ciendaine,
  Ici wanyitania ituĩke ũmwe.

2.Nawe Mwendani mamenyagĩrĩre
  Matũũrage makwĩgiritĩtie;
  Makiragĩrĩrie o metereire
  Na wĩtĩkio ũtangĩenyenyeka.

3.ũmaiyũragĩrie gĩkeno kĩrĩa
  Kĩrĩmaninagĩra kĩeha thĩ;
  Ũmaikaragie o matukũ mothe,
  Muoyo wao mature mendaine.

                   1 Joh 4:16-21
