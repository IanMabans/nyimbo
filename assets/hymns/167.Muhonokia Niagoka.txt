Mũhonokia Nĩagoka(129)
(The Saviour is coming)

1.Mũhonokia nĩagoka;
  Nĩtũgakena mũno:
  Rĩu tũtũire tũrĩraga
  Nĩ ũndũ wa ũũru witũ.
          Twakũhoya:
          Hiũha,Mũhonokia;
          Twakũhoya:
          Hiũha,Mũhonokia;

2.Tene rĩrĩa wokĩte thĩ
  Warĩ ta mũndũ mũkĩa;
  Ningĩ rĩrĩa ũgacoka
  Nĩũgoka na hinya.

3.Ndũkae gũikara mũno
  Hiũha rĩu gũcoka thĩ;
  Ũka ũtũtware gwaku
  Mwagoinĩ wa Igũrũ.

           Kũg 22:20
