Rĩrĩa Jesũ Agacoka Agere managĩ(283)
(When he cometh, when he cometh)

1.Rĩrĩa Jesũ agacoka
  Agere managĩ,
  Na managĩ nĩmo andũ
  Arĩa aanakũũra,

        Magathera ta njata
        O ta ya kĩwariĩ,
        Noguo magathakara,
        Managĩ make.

2.Akongania, akongania,
  Amatware gwake,
  Arĩa ega na atheru,
  Arĩa anakũũra.

3.Twana tutu, twana tutu,
  Mwende Mũkũũri,
  Inyuĩ nĩ Inyuĩ managĩ,
  Managĩ make.

                    Mal 3:17
