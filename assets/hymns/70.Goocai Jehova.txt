Goocai Jehova(57)
(Ye Holy Angels Bright)

1.Goocai Jehova, mũgoocagĩreni
  Kũrĩa gũtũgĩru:mũmũnenehagie,
  Inyuothe araika ake,inyuĩ ita ciake ciothe.

2.Rĩĩtwa rĩa Jehova nĩrĩkagĩrĩrũo
  Nĩ ciũmbe na andũ aria atũũragia:
  Amu watho wake noguo watũmire igĩe ho.

3.Athamaki a thĩ,na acirithania,
  Inyuĩ andũ othe,ethĩ o na akũrũ,
  Kumagiani riri wake ũrĩa wĩ Igũrũ o na thĩ.

4.Tondũ atheu ake nĩamatũgĩrĩtie,
  Gĩtĩo o na ũgooci nĩimwagĩrĩire;
  Inyuĩ mũmũkuhĩrĩirie ganai rĩĩtwa rĩake.

                   Thab 148
