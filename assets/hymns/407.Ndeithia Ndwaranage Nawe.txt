Ndeithia, Ndwaranage Nawe(315)
(O Master Let me walk with thee)

1.Ndeithia, ndwaranage nawe,
  Na ndungatage ndĩnyihĩtie;
  ‘He hitho yaku, ‘ndeithagia
  Hatĩkainĩ ya mĩhang’o.

2.Arĩa mahũthĩte ngoro
  Ndeithia ndĩmaarahũrage;
  Arĩa mahĩtĩtie njĩra
  Ndĩmonagĩrĩrie gwaku.

3.Ndutaga gũkirĩrĩria
  Tũgĩthiĩ thiritũ nawe;
  Ũ’he wĩtĩkio mũrũmu
  Wa gũtoria ũũru wothe.

4.Ngĩe na wĩrĩgĩrĩro
  Wa kũnyonaga ũtheri;
  Ndĩ na thayũ waku, Mwathi,
  Reke tũtũũranie nawe.

              Joh 13:12-17
