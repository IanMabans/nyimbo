Kwĩ Mũciĩ Mũthaka
(There is a City Bright)

1.Kwĩ mũciĩ mũthaka
  Ũtaganagĩrwo:
  Ũũru gũtirĩ,
  Ũũru gũtirĩ,
  Ũngĩgatonya kuo.

2.Mũrũ wa Ngai, nĩwe
  Mwega na mũtheru:
  Ũ’thambie ngoro,
  Ũ’thambie ngoro,
  ‘Hote gũkinya kũu.

3.Ngwenda gũgwĩtĩkia,
  Nawe ũnjathage:
  ‘He Roho waku,
  ‘He Roho waku,
  Nduĩke mwega tawe.

4.Nĩguo wanjiyĩra
  Ũhote kũndwara
  Ndĩ o mũtheru,
  Ndĩ o mũtheru,
  Mũciĩ ũcio mwega.

                 Kũg 22:14
