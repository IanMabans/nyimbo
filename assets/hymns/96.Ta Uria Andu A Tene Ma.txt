Ta ũrĩa Andũ A Tene Ma

1.Ta ũrĩa andũ a tene ma
  Makenire mona njata,
  Magĩtongorio nĩ ũtheri,
  O ta ũguo,Ngai,tũtongorie.

       Mwathani,Jesũ Kristũ,
       Tũteithie tũũke harĩ We,
       Tũkũgooce na ngoro ciitũ.
       Nĩwe Mũthamaki wĩtu.

2.Magĩthiĩ makenete,
  Magĩkinya harĩ Jesũ,
  Makĩmũgooca na ngoro;
  Tũteithie,Ngai,twĩke ũguo.

3.Makĩmũhe indo njega,
  Magĩciiga mũharatĩinĩ,
  Reke tũmũhe ngoro citũ,
  Na indo ciothe twĩ nacio.

         Mat 2:1-11
