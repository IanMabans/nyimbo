Mũthamaki wa wendani(171)
(The King of love my shepherd is)

1.Mũthamaki wa wendani
  Nĩwe Mũrĩithi wakwa;
  Ndingiaga kĩndũ ndĩ nake,
  Nake atũũranie na niĩ.

2.Nĩwe ũrĩndongoragia,
  Akanginyĩria nyeki;
  Na kũndũ kwega kũruru
  Ahũnagie ngoro yakwa.

3.Ndĩ mũkuruinĩ wa gĩkuũ
  Ndingĩĩtigĩra ũndũ;
  Akĩ’nyitaga na guoko
  Nginye mũtharabainĩ.

4.Rĩmwe nĩndĩmũtigaga,
  Nowe ndandirikaga;
  Aka’hĩmbĩria na moko
  Andware gwake Igũrũ.

5.Wega waku ndũgathira
  Matukũ makwa mothe:
  Mũrĩithi mwega ngũtũũra
  Nawe mĩndĩ o na mĩndĩ.

              Joh 10
