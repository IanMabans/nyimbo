Rũgendoinĩ rũrũ rwa gũthiĩ Igũrũ(236)
(When you start for the Land)

1.Rũgendoinĩ rũrũ rwa guthiĩ Igũrũ
  Twarana na Mwathi Jesũ;
  Mogwati nĩ maingĩ, ndũngĩthiĩ wĩ wiki,
  Twarana na Mwathi Jesũ.

        Twarana nake, twarana nake ,
        Twarana na Mwathi Jesũ;
        Gwakorũo nĩ ũtukũ kana nĩ mũthenya,
        Twarana na Mwathi Jesũ.

2.Rũgendoinĩ rwa gũthiĩ Igũrũ nĩ rũraihu
  Twarana na Mwathi Jesũ;
  Nĩtũkahurũka twarĩkia rũgendo,
  Twarana na Mwathi Jesũ.

3.Nĩtũthondekeirũo ciikaro Igũrũ
  Twarana na Mwathi Jesũ;
  Twakinya Igũrũ nĩtũkona Mwathani,
  Twarana na Mwathi Jesũ;

4.Twatigia hanini twambate Igũrũ
  Twarana na Mwathi Jesũ;
  Nĩkuo tũgatũũra tene o na tene,
  Twarana na Mwathi Jesũ;


            Joh 8:12
