Igua Kanua Ka Jesũ Mũkũũri(262)
(with thou be made whole)

1.Igua kanua ka Jesũ Mũkũũri witũ:
  E na wendo mũingĩ na kĩeha giitũ
  Tũngĩrũara ngoro atũthondeka,
  Ningĩ tũngĩtĩkia atũteithia.
              Ũkani o rĩu, andũ othe aitũ,
              Nawe mũrũaru ngoroinĩ yaku,
              Mũhonio nĩ Jesũ na thakame yake;
              Ũkani ũmũthĩ mũtuĩke ake.

2.Nĩ mũgambo wa Jesũ ũgũtwĩta rĩu;
  Tũmũkuhĩrĩrie na ngoro ciitũ.
  Na ũngĩenda kũhona, ũheo muoyo
  Wĩrire ũmũthĩ ũtherio ngoro.

3.Tiga guoya wa andũ: ũngĩthekererũo.
  Nĩũkwendwo nĩ Jesũ, na ndũkarumwo
  Tiga kũgonderera; ĩtĩkia rĩu,
  Ũgatonya mũciĩ wa Ngai Igũrũ.


                 Mar 10:39, 40
