Nĩekũndongoria Mwathani,
( He Leadeth Me)

1.Nĩekũndongoria Mwathani,
  Na niĩ nĩngũkena mũno;
  Harĩa hothe ndĩĩthiaga,
  Nĩ Jesũ ũrĩndongoragia.
      Nĩandongoragia Mwathani,
      Guoko gwake kũ’nyitĩte,
      Nĩngũgĩtwarana nake,
      Mwathani, andongoragie.

2.Na rĩmwe ndĩ magerioinĩ,
  Na rĩngĩ ndĩ gĩkenoinĩ;
  Marĩa mothe marĩũkaga,
  Mwathani, ũndeithagie.

3.Guoko gwake kũ’nyitĩte,
  Amwe nĩmekũrakara;
  Kĩrĩa gĩothe arĩ’heaga,
  Ndĩ hakuhĩ kwamũkĩra.

4.Ingĩnina wĩra wa thĩ,
  Nĩngathiĩ gwake Igũrũ;
  Na niĩ nĩngamũkumagia,
  We ũrĩa wanandongoria.


          Thab 139:10, 23 ,24
