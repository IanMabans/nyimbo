ũtukũ Macemanĩtie(117)
(The Strive is O’er)

1.ũtukũ macemanĩtie
  Me na guoya ,Jesũ agĩũka
  Akĩmera,”Gĩai na thayũ!”
    Haleluya!!

2.Toma aaigua nĩ mũriũku
  Akiuga,”Niĩ ndingĩĩtĩkia!
  No mũhaka ngeyonera.”
    Haleluya!!

3.”Wee Toma,githĩ ndũkuona
  Irema cia moko makwa,
  Magũrũ o na mbaruinĩ?”
    Haleluya!!

4.O rĩmwe akĩmũgooca
  Akĩmwĩra,”Ndĩ gwĩtĩkia,
  Mwathani wakwa,Ngai wakwa!
   “Haleluya!!

5.Na maacemania Galili
  Jesũ witũ nĩaamerire,
  “Thiĩi mũkahunjie ũhoro.”
    “Haleluya!!

6.Na ũhonokio nĩ wao
  Arĩa othe metĩkagia
  Mateyoneire na maitho.
  Haleluya.


              Joh 20:19-28
