Gwĩ Gĩkeno Kĩnene ma! Jesũ(120)
(joy to the world the lord is come)

1.Gwĩ gĩkeno kĩnene ma!
  Jesũ nĩokire thĩ
  Andũ othe nĩmaine,
  Mamũhe ngoro ciao,
  Mamũhe ngoro ciao,
  Mamũhe ngoro ciao rĩu.

2.Gwĩ gĩkeno kĩnene ma!
  Jesũ nĩatoretie
  Ũthamaki wa Caitani,
  Ũthamaki wa Caitani.
  Na nĩũgateo nĩwe,
  Na nĩũgateo nĩwe,
  Na nĩũgateo nĩwe wothe.

3.Gwĩ gĩkeno kĩnene ma!
  Jesũ nĩaaninire
  Kĩrumi kĩa andũ ake
  Kĩrumi kĩa andũ ake
  Na thakame yake,
  Na thakame yake,
  Na thakame yake iiki.

4.Gwĩ gĩkeno kĩnene ma!
  Jesũ e na hinya;
  Nĩagatũriũkia
  Nĩagatũriũkia
  Tũtũũre Igũrũ,
  Tũtũũre Igũrũ,
  Tũtũũre Igũrũ nake.

           Thab 86:9-13
