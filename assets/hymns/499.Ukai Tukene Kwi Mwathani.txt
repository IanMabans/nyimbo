ũkai Tũkene Kwĩ Mwathani
(Twana tũthiũrũrũke tũkĩina)

1.ũkai tũkenere Mwathani,
  He Mwathani, he Mwathani,
  Ũkai tũkenere Mwathani,
  Mũthenya wake wa gĩkeno.
 (Twana tũrore igũrũ, Tũkĩhũra hĩ)

2.ũkai tũgoocage Mwathani,
  Tũgooce Jesũ, tũgooce Jesũ,
  Ũkai tũgoocage Mwathani,
  Mũthenya wake wa Gĩkeno.
(Twana tũhinge maitho na moko)

3.ũkai tũhoyage Mwathani,
  Atũteithie tũikarage nake,
  Ũkai tũhoyage nake,
  Mũthenya wake wa Gĩkeno.
