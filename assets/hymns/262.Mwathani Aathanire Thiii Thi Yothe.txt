Mwathani Aathanire:
 Thiĩi Thĩ Yothe(205)

1.Mwathani aathanire, “Thiĩi thĩ yothe,
  Mũgatũme andũ a ndũrĩrĩ ciothe,
  Matuĩke arutwo akwa.”

           Nũ ũgũthiĩ akoimbũre Jesũ,
           Aanĩrĩre ũhoro ũyũ Mwega:
           Oigire, “ũthamaki wa Igũrũ Nĩũkuhĩhĩirie?”

2.Kĩrĩndĩ gĩa thĩ ĩno gĩtuĩke gĩake;
  Jesũ aambirũo mũtĩ igũrũ;
  Agĩthĩnio mũno.

3.Nũ ũkũiguĩra tha ngiri icio nyingĩ
  Cia andũ ma ndumainĩ mamenye Jesũ
  Nĩamakuĩrĩire?

4.Ngai, wendo waku gũkũ ũhingio
  Nĩ andũ othe aku;moige,”Amen,
  Jesũ nĩahunjio.”

                  Mat 28:19,20
