Mũkũũri Witũ Mwega Ma(140)
(Our Blessed Redeemer)

1.Mũkũũri witũ mwega ma
  Atanathiĩ gwake,
  Nĩ aatũheire Mũteithia,
  Roho wake.

2.Okire na ũhoreri
  Atũranie na ithuĩ;
  Thayũ o na wendo wake
   Agĩtũhe.

3.Roho ũcio wa wendani
  Nĩ wa gũtũteithia,
  Tũngĩmuonera gĩikaro
    Ngoro thĩinĩ.

4.Na kũigua nĩtũiguaga
  Mũgambo mwega ma
  Wa Roho agĩtwarĩria
  Ngoro thĩinĩ.

5.Maũndũ mothe ma ũthingu
  Na  gũtoria guothe
  Nĩ Roho ũtũheaga
   Ngoro thĩinĩ.

6.Mũhorerania Mũtheru
  Tũteithagie twĩ thĩ,
  Ngoro ciitũ ciikarage
  Irĩ ciaku.

           Joh 20:22
