Jesũ Nĩendaga Nduĩke
(Jesus wants me for a sunbeam)

1.Jesũ nĩendaga nduĩke
  Ta mũrũri wa riũa;
  Ndĩmũtheragĩre wega,
  Nĩguo akenage.

            Mĩrũri, mĩrũri,
            Jesũ nĩendaga mĩrũri;
            Mĩrũri, mĩrũri,
            Tũrĩ mĩrũri yake.

2.Jesũ nĩendaga nduĩke
  Wa kwendana na andũ;
  Ndĩmonagie wega mũno
  Ũrĩa angenagia.

3.Nĩndĩhoyaga Jesũ
  Anjikaragie ngoro;
  Nĩguo njage waganu
  Ũngĩgiria ‘there.

4.Nĩĩ ndĩ mũrũri wa Jesũ,
  Ndĩmũtheragĩre;
  Na nĩngathiĩ kwa Jesũ,
  Ndũre hamwe nake.

              Mat 15:6
