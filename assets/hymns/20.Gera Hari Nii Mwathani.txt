Gera Harĩ Niĩ Mwathani
(Come by here my lord)

1.Gera harĩ niĩ Mwathani,
  Gera harĩ niĩ Mwathani,
  Gera harĩ niĩ Mwathani,
  Ndagũthaitha Mwathani.

2.Ndũkahĩtũke Mwathani,
  Ndũkahĩtũke Mwathani,
  Ndũkahĩtũke Mwathani,
  Ndagũthaitha Mwathani.

3.Njiguĩra tha Wee Mwathani,
  Njiguĩra tha Wee Mwathani,
  Njiguĩra tha Wee Mwathani,
  Ndagũthaitha Mwathani.

4.Tonya ngoroinĩ Mwathani,
  Tonya ngoroinĩ Mwathani,
  Tonya ngoroinĩ Mwathani,
  Ndagũthaitha Mwathani.

5.Ndũkanandige Mwathani,
  Ndũkanandige Mwathani,
  Ndũkanandige Mwathani,
  Ndagũthaitha Mwathani.


                  Luk 17:11-19
