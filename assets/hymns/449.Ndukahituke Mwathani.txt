Ndũka’hĩtũke, Mwathani(351)
(Pass Me Not O Gentle Saviour)

1. Ndũkahĩtũke, Mwathani,Ũnjiguĩre tha;
  Ũgĩceerera arĩa angĩ Ndũkahĩtũke.

         Jesũ, Jesũ, Ũnjiguĩre tha;
         Ũgĩceerera arĩa angĩ Ndũka’hĩtũke.

2.Ngũũka gĩtĩinĩ kĩu gĩa tha Nyone ũhuruko;
  Ndurĩtie ndu ‘thuthĩkĩte, Ndeithia gwĩtĩkia.

3.Ndĩĩhokete wega waku Nyone ũthiu waku;
  ‘Honia ironda ngoro yakwa Na ũ’honokie.

4.Nĩwe gĩthima gĩa thayũ Ngoroinĩ yakwa;
  Ndirĩ na ũngĩ gũkũ thĩ ĩno Kana matuinĩ.

                  Luk 17:11-19
