Jehova Nĩerire Nuhu(358)

1.Jehova nĩerire Nuhu, “Wĩakĩre thabina;
  Yarĩka, ũmĩtonye thĩinĩ nĩguo ngũhonokie.”

          Ndũgĩũke, ngũgĩũke ũtonye,
          Mũrango ti mũhinge;
          Na wahingwo ndũkahingũrũo,
          Ndũgĩũke ũtonye!

2.Yarĩka Nuhu nĩaamerire, makĩmũthekerera
  Makiuga nĩ wĩra wa ũrimũ, makĩrega othe.

3.Jesũ nĩwe thabina iitũ, mũrango nĩũkũhingwo,
  Tonya rĩu ndũkahingĩrĩrio, na nĩũkũhonokio.

4.ũikarĩte nja kana thĩinĩ, kana wĩ mũromoinĩ?
  Wacũthĩrĩria mĩena yerĩ nĩũkũhingĩrĩrio.

                   Kĩam 6-8
