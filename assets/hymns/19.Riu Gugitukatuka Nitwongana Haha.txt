Rĩu Gũgĩtukatuka Nĩtwongana Haha(15)
(As Darker,Darker Fall Around)

1.Rĩu gũgĩtukatuka nĩtwongana haha;
  Tũgũthathaiye na nyĩmbo ũtũhe ũtheri.

2.Ithe witũ wa Igũrũ ,Nĩũũĩ mathĩna
  Marĩa mothe me ngoroinĩ na maithori maitũ.

3.Twakũhoya ririkana arata aitũ othe
  Me gũkuhĩ na kũraya,othe ũmarathime.

4.Andũ othe arĩa anogu o na arĩa magũĩte,
  Arĩa arwaru na arĩa agerie,othe ĩmarathime.

5.Ngai wa tha na wa wendo nĩtwakũnengera
  Metanga maitũ maritũ , tũigue twakũhoya.
