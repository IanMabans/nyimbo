Nĩtũthathaiye Mwathani Jesũ(61)
(Oh Worship the King)

1.Nĩtũthathaiye Mwathani Jesũ
  Muthamaki wa Igũrũ kwa Ngai
  Nĩ Mũtũgitĩri,ningĩ nowe Ngo,
  Nĩtũmũgoocage na ngoro ciitũ.

2.Anĩrĩrai ũhoti wake,
  O na ũtugi ũtangĩthimĩka;
  Aiyũire guothe,namo marurumĩ
  Nĩmo monanagia mahinya make.

3.Mawĩra maku ma magegania
  Mamemerekagia ngumo yaku;
  Nĩwe wahandire mũthingi wa thĩ,
  Ũkĩmĩirigĩra na maĩ ma iria.

4.Njata na ĩrĩma nĩciumbũraga
  Wendo waku ũtangĩĩranĩka:
  Mahũa,mĩtĩ,ireera na mbura,
  Ciothe cionanagia ũtugi waku.

5.Tũtirĩ hinya,tũrĩ o rũkũngũ,
  Tũkwĩhokete wee Hinya witũ:
  Matha maku mothe nĩũtũkinyĩirie,
  Wee Mũrata witũ,na Mũtũkũũri.

6.ũhoti,wendo,itangĩĩranĩka
  Araika makũinagĩra Igũrũ;
  Ciũmbe ciothe nyinyi o na iria nene
  Igagũkũngũĩra na gũkũgooca.

              Thab 104
