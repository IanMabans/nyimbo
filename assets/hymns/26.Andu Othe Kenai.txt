Andũ Othe kenai
(Rejoice the Lord is King)

1. Andũ othe kenai
     Mũgathe Mwathani,
     Mũthamaki wanyu;
    Mũmũkũngũire.

             Ngoro na mĩgambo yanyu,
             Ciothe imĩkenagĩre.
2.Jesũ Mũhonokia
    Ngai wa ma na wendo,
    A'tũtheria mehia,
    Rĩu nĩ Mũthamaki.

3.Jesũ nĩ Mwathani
   wa thĩ na Igũrũ,
   Nĩahotete gĩkuũ;
   Mũtoria mũnene.

4.Aikaire guokoinĩ
    kwa ũrĩo kwa Ngai,
    O kinya thũ ciake,
    Imũinamĩrĩre.

             Filip 4:4
