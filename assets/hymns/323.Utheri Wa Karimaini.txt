ũtheri Wa Karĩmainĩ(257)
(There is sun Light on the Hilltop)

1.ũtheri wa karĩmainĩ
  Ũiyũrĩte thĩ yothe,
  Mĩrũri ĩmũrĩkĩte
  Kũrĩa kũruru wega;
  Na ũtheri ũcio noguo
  Ũiyũire ngoro yakwa,
  Tondũ Mwathani e thĩinĩ
  Ta mĩrũri ya riũa.

         Ũkengi mwega wa Jesũ
         Ũ’mũrĩkĩte ngoro:
         Jesũ andiirĩra gĩthithi
         Kĩeha no gĩgathira.

2.ũtheri wa karĩmainĩ
  Ũiyũrĩte thĩ yothe,
  Mwathani njakĩria tawa
  Na ũ’ninĩre nduma:
  Ndanina rũgendo rwakwa
  Na wĩra wakwa wothe
  Nĩngakenaga Igũrũ
  Na ngũinamagĩrĩre.

3.Ngũgũithia kĩeha tĩriinĩ
  O ta nguo ndembũku,
  Ũ’humbe gĩkeno gĩaku
  ‘Hotage gũgũkumia:
  Mũciĩ ũcio wa Igũrũ
  Kũu wendaga njũke,
  Meciria ma ngoro yakwa
  Nĩmaũcũthĩrĩirie.

4.Mwathani nĩ ũngũũrĩte
  O hamwe na indo ciakwa,
  Tawa ũcio ũnjakĩire
  Nĩ wa gũkũgoocithia,
  Nĩguo nyonie andũ othe
  Atĩ wĩ Mũhonokia:
  Mwathani, ũrogoocagwo
  Nginya tene na tene.


          2 Kor 4:3-6
