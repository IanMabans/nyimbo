Thiĩ na mbere,Mũthamaki(94)
(Ride On,Ride On in Majesty)

1.Thiĩ na mbere,Mũthamaki,
  Ũkĩinagĩrũo Hosana,
  We ũhaicĩte ndigiri
  Ũkĩaragĩrũo makĩndũ.

2.Thiĩ na mbere,Mũthamaki,
  Wĩnyihĩtie nginya gũkua:
  Ta ambĩrĩria rĩu,we Kristũ,
  Gũtongoria gĩkuũ na mehia.

3.Thiĩ na mbere,Mũthamaki,
  Araika nĩmetereire,
  Magegete me na kĩeha,
  Werekeire gũtũkuĩra.

4.Thiĩ na mbere,Mũthamaki,
  Mahiũrania me hakuhĩ,
  Thoguo aikaire gĩtĩinĩ
  Kĩa ũnene agwetereire.

5.Thiĩ na mbere,Mũthamaki,
  Kunda ruo wĩnyihĩtie;
  Goiya ngingo ũtuĩkane,
  Ũthamakage Ngai witũ.


             Joh 12:12-19
