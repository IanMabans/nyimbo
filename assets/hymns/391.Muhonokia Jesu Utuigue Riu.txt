Mũhonokia Jesũ ũtũigue Rĩu(301)
(Break thou the bread of life)

1.Mũhonokia Jesũ ũtũigue rĩu,
  Tũgĩkũhoya irio cia muoyo;
  Tũtingĩiganio nĩ indo cia thĩ,
  Nowe wiki ũtũhũnagia ma.

2.Ndeto ciaku Jesũ nĩ ndathime,
  Itherie ngoro cia andũ aku,
  Nĩ getha tũmenye wĩ na hinya
  Wa gũtuonia njĩra yaku ya ma.

3.Ndakũhoya rĩu Roho waku
  Andeithagie gũkũmenya wega,
  Nĩ getha nduĩke ma mwana waku.
  Tondũ wa Mũtũhonokia Jesũ.

4.Tũiyũrie ngoro rĩu ta ũrĩa tene
  Andũ a Galili maaiyũririo;
  Tũkwendage kũna nginya rĩrĩa
  Ũgatũiyĩra tũũke harĩ we.

                Joh 6:1-14
