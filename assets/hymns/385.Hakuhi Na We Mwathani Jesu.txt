Hakuhĩ Na We Mwathani Jesũ
(let me come closer to thee, Lord Jesus)

1.Hakuhĩ na we, Mwathani Jesũ,
  Ngucia njikarage ho,
  Reke ‘menyage gwĩtirĩrĩria,
  Na we o we wiki.

2.Nĩngwenda, Jesũ ũkengi waku,
  Ware thĩinĩ wakwa,
  Kiugo o giothe nĩkĩonanagie
  O wendani waku.

3.Tũma ngoro ĩtuĩke gĩthima
  Kĩa maĩ maku, Jesũ,
  Matherũkagĩre arĩa anyotu,
  Na we ũgoocagwo!

4.ũka, Mwathani, ũthamakagĩre
  Ngoroinĩ yakwa,
  Nduĩkage ngombo ya kwĩhokeka
  Ũndũinĩ wothe.

5.No we, Mwathani wiki hũtagĩra,
  No we nyotagĩra.
  Ngĩĩrirĩria ndonye mũciĩ waku,
  Kũrĩa kwĩ na hũni.

                      Thab 17:8
