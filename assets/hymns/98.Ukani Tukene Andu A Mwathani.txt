ũkani Tũkene,Andũ A Mwathani(73)
(O come All Ye Faithful)

1.ũkani tũkene,andũ a Mwathani,
  Nĩtũthiĩi kũu Bethilehemu;
  Nĩaciarĩtwo Muhonokia witũ:

        Ũkani tũmũgathe,
        Ũkani tũmugathe,
        Ũkani tũmũgathe
        Mwathi Jesũ.

2.Nake nĩwe Ngai,na nowe ũtheri,
  Na ndaaregire gũtuĩka gakenge;
  Nĩko Mwathani,gaka gaciarĩtwo:

3.Mũtimũkumie,inyuĩ araika,
  Inyuothe mũtũgagĩrie Mwathi witũ;
  Ngai arogoocwo,o Igũrũ mũno:

4.Mwathani witũ,twagũkũngũĩra
  Jesũ ũheagwo ũgooci wothe.
  Nĩwe ũhoro, ũtuĩkĩte mũndũ:

                    Luk 2:15
