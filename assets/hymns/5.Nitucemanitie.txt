Nĩtũcemanĩtie (4)
(We Have Met Together in thy house)

1.Nĩtũcemanĩtie
  Gũkũ nyũmba yaku;
  ũgomane na ithuĩ,
  Mwathani Ngai witũ.

2.Tũtonyete nĩkĩ
  Haha mbere yaku?
  Nĩ tũigue na tũnyite
  Ndeto ciaku njega.

3.Nĩkĩo tũtonyete,
  Atĩ tũgũthaithe,
  Na tumbũre wĩhia witũ;
  We, Ngai, ũtuohere.

4.Tũtiagĩrĩirũo
  Tũkũinamĩrĩre:
  Nĩtũgũconoka mũno
  Nĩwe Ngai mũnene.

5.Ithe witũ mwega
  ũtũiguĩre tha;
  Tũrathime ithuothe,
  Nĩtwagũthaitha.


           Mat 18:20
