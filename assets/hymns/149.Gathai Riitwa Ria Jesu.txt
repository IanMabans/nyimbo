Gathai Rĩĩtwa Rĩa Jesũ(118)
(All Hail the power of Jesus Name)

1.Gathai rĩĩtwa rĩa Jesũ;
  Araika o nao
  Mamũgathe,na magooce
  Mũthamaki witũ.

2.Ithuĩ twanateithio
  Tũtige waganu:
  Kenai nĩ ũndũ wa wendo
  Wa Mwathi witũ.

3.Ndũrĩrĩ ciothe iria
  Itũire gũkũ thĩ.
  Mũtikenere thakame
  Na tha cia Mũkũũri.

4.Jesũ aacoka gũkũ thĩ
  Atuĩke Mũciri,
  Nĩagatuo Mũthamaki
  Wa andũ othe a thĩ.

5.Twakinya mũciĩ ũrĩa
  Atũire Igũrũ,
  Twĩ hamwe na andũ othe kuo,
  Tũkagooca Jesũ.

                   Kũg 7:9-11
