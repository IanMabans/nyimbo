Rĩrĩa Nguona Mũtharaba (108)
(When I survey the wondrous cross)

1.Rĩrĩa nguona mũtharaba
  Harĩa Kristũ aanguĩrĩire,
  Makwa me bata no tũhũ,
  Mwĩtĩo ngaũnyarara.

2.Mwathani ngiria kwĩgooca
  Tiga na gĩkuũ kĩu kĩa Jesũ;
  Marĩa mothe marĩngucagia
  Ndĩmaigage thakame-inĩ.

3.Kĩeha na wendo igĩitĩka
  Kuuma mũtwe na magũrũ;
  Kĩeha wendo ciaiguana,
  Na mĩigua ĩgĩtumwo thũmbĩ.

4.Thĩ yothe ĩngĩrĩ yakwa
  Tikĩo kĩheo kĩiganu;
  Wendo mũnene ta ũcio
  Ũnjĩtagia muoyo na mwĩrĩ.

               Gal 6:14
