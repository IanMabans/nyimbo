ũthamaki Waku(249)
(Thy Kingdom come o God)

1.ũthamaki waku
  Ũũke gũkũ gwitũ,
  Na ũtũninĩre
  Watho wa Caitani.

2.Wendo o na thayũ
  Waku ũũke gwitũ;
  Kũmenana gũkũ
  Gũtigwo nĩ andũ.

3.Hingo ĩgoka-rĩ
  Mbaara ĩgathira?
  Na maũru mothe
  Matigwo nĩ andũ?

4.Jesũ twakũhoya
  Ũũke na hinya ma:
  Ũtũhotithagie
  Tũtorie Caitani.

5.Rĩĩtwa rĩake Jesũ
  Rĩtiendagwo ma;
  Na andũ aingĩ
  Me mehiainĩ.

6.Na kũndũ kũingĩ
  Gũtũire o nduma;
  Tũteithagie Jesũ
  Tũtuĩke ũtheri.

            Luk 11:2, 20-22
