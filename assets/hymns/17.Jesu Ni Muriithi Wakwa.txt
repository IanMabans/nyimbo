Jesũ Nĩ Mũrĩithi wakwa(333)
(Jesus Tender shepherd hear me)

1.Jesũ nĩ Mũrĩithi wakwa,
  Ndĩ kagondu kanini;
  ũndaarie ũtukũ ũyũ,
  ũnginyĩrie kĩroko.

2.ũmũthĩ nĩũndeithĩtie;
  Ngũgũcokeria ngatho:
  Ningĩ nĩũ'menyereire,
  Na ũkandĩithia ta ng'ondi.

3.ũ'thengerie ũũru wothe,
  Ningĩ ũ'thambie ngoro;
  ũrathime andũ othe
  Arĩa twendaine nao.

4.Nĩguo rĩrĩa ũkanjĩta
  Nyume gũkũ thĩ ĩno,
  ũkanjĩtĩkĩria ndũũre
  Gwaku tene na tene.

             Thab 4:8
