Tũrathime Rĩu, Jesũ(341)
(Saviour, Breath an evening blessing)

1.Tũrathime rĩu, jesũ,
  Nĩ ihinda rĩa gũkoma;
  Tũkuumbũra ũũru witũ:
  We, Mwathani, tuohere.

2.Mbara ĩngĩkuhĩrĩria
  We no ũtũgitĩre;
  Na ũgwati ũngĩtũkora
  Ndũngĩaga kũwona.

3.O na nduma ĩngĩneneha
  Tũtingĩhithwo nĩyo;
  Andũ aku ũikaragia,
  Na ndũngĩya gũcũnga.

4.ũtukũ rĩrĩa twĩ toro
  Ũngĩenda gũtwĩta,
  No ũtũtware gwaku mũciĩ
  Tũgatũũranie nawe.

                Thab 91
