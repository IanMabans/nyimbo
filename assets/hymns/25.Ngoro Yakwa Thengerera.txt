Ngoro yakwa Thengerera (19)
(Hark the Glad Sound)

1.Ngoro yakwa thengerera
  Gĩtĩinĩ Kĩu gĩa tha
  Wĩnyihĩtie harĩ Jesũ,
  Mwaranĩrie nake.

2.Caitani nĩanguithĩtie
  Mũrigo wa mehia;
  Kwĩ na mbaara o na guoya
  Mwathani  'hurũkia.

3.Nĩ ũndũ wa ciĩrĩro ciaku
  Nĩngũũka harĩ we
  Ndĩ mũritũhĩre ngoro,
  Mwathani 'hurũkia.

4.Tuĩka ngo o na kĩhitho
  Ndĩĩgitie harĩ we,
  'Hotage kwĩra Caitani
  Wee nĩwanguĩrĩire.

5.Wendo waku nĩ mũnene;
  Nĩwetĩkĩrire
  Kwambwo o mũtharabainĩ
  Nĩguo nga'honoka.

                   Ahib 10:19-23
