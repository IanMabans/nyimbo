Jesũ, Mũhonokia wakwa(190)
(Precious Saviour Thou Hast Saved)

1.Jesũ, Mũhonokia wakwa,
  Rĩu ndĩ waku wiki,
  Thakame yaku nĩĩ’theragia,
  Jesũ we Gatũrũme!

           Nĩtũgũkũgooca,Jesũ,
           Jesũ Gatũrũme ka Ngai,
           Thakame yaku ĩĩtheragia,
           Ndakũgooca Mwathani!

2.Ndatũire ndĩĩgũmagĩra,
  Ngĩcaria kĩhurũko;
  Rĩu ndirĩ na ũndũ ũngĩ,
  Tiga kwĩhoka Jesũ.

3.Ndĩĩheanaga cia Jesũ,
  Ndĩ na ũcamba wothe;
  Tondũ nĩaanjohorire,
  Na akĩ’honia mahia.

5.Ngũgooca Mũkũũri wakwa,
  Na nowe Mũ’honokia;
  Jesũ Mũgitĩri wakwa,
  Hingo ciothe nĩagoocwo!

             Kũg 7:10
