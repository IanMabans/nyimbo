Mwathani Wakwa, ũnjikarie(335)
(Abide with me, fast falls the eventide)

1.Mwathani wakwa, ũnjikarie
  Tondũ rĩu gũkirie gũtuka:
  O na ateithia angĩ mangĩndiga,
  Wee, Mũndeithia mwega, ũnjikarie.

2.Matukũ maitũ nĩmathiraga;
  Indo cia gũkũ itirĩ bata;
  Nayo mĩago ndĩtũkenagia;
  Wee, ũtegarũraga, ũnjikarie.

3.Nĩngwenda Roho waku Mũtheru
  Aikarage ngoroinĩ yakwa;
  Hingo ya thĩna kana mũtino,
  Mũtongoria wakwa, ũnjikarie.

4.Caitani ndangĩhota kũ’nyita
  Na mĩrimũ kana magerio:
  O na ndingĩĩtigĩra gũkua,
  Wee, Mwene-muoyo, no ũnjikarie.

5.Tene ndatũire o ndumainĩ,
  Ndiamenyete ũhoro waku:
  Rĩu nĩnjũĩ nĩwanguĩrĩire;
  Mũhonokia wakwa, ũnjikarie.


                 Luk 24:29
