Atheru Arĩa Mahurũkĩte(277)
(For all the saints)

1.Atheru arĩa mahurũkĩte
  Na nĩmoimbũrĩte ngumo yake,
  Tondũ wao Jesũ arokumio:

                  Thathaiya Ngai;
                  Thathaiya Ngai.

2.We ihiga nowe mehokete,
  Mũnene wao na njamba ya ita,
  Nowe ũtheri marĩ ndumainĩ:

3.Athigari aku ehokeku
  Marorwaga ta arĩa a mbere
  Nĩguo makona thũmbĩ ya muoyo:

4.Mũthenya wĩ mwago nĩũrĩkinya,
  Atheru mariũke mbũtũ nene,
  Mwene riri amatongoretie:

5.Makoima ituri ciothe cia thĩ,
  Matonye itũũra rĩrĩa rĩtheru
  Macanjamũkĩte makĩĩnaga.

               Luk 13:29
