’Heeherera,Roho, Na Mĩhũmũ Yaku(138)
(Breathe on me Breathe of God)

1.’Heeherera,Roho,
  Na mĩhũmũ yaku;
  Njiyũragia muoyo mwerũ
  Nyendanage tawe.

2.‘Heeherera,Roho,
  Na mĩhũmũ yaku;
  ‘Heaga hinya njĩkage
  Ciĩko o ta ciaku.

3.’Heeherera, Roho,
  Na mĩhũmũ yaku;
  Nginya ngoro ĩgĩthere
  Njĩke kwenda gwaku.

4.’Heeherera, Roho,
  Na mĩhũmũ yaku;
  ‘Hotithagia hĩndĩ ciothe
  Gũkiragĩrĩria.

5.’Heeherera, Roho,
  Na mĩhũmũ yaku;
  Nginya mwĩrĩ wakwa wothe
  Ũkengagio nĩwe.

6.’Heeherera, Roho,
  Na mĩhũmũ yaku;
  Nĩguo ngatũrania na we
  Tene o na tene.

              Joh 20:22
