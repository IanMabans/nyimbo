Nĩtũthiĩni Ita(233)
(Onward Christian Soldiers)

1.Nĩtũthiĩni ita, Aanake aya,
  Tũthiĩ na Jesũ, Nĩegũtũtwara!
  Ĩni, Mũthamaki Nĩwe Kristũ;
  Tũgũtongorio nĩ ũrĩa ũtahootagwo.

          Nĩtũthiĩ ita, Aanake aya:
          Jesũ mwene nĩwe ũgũtũtwara.

2.Ita cia Caitani Rĩrĩa ciamuona
  Ikũũra o rĩmwe, Ikahooteka:
  Haiya! Andũ aitũ, Nĩtũciingate:
  Ningĩ Jesũ Kristũ nake Tũmũkumie!

3.Kanitha wa Kristũ ũhuana ita;
  Andũ a Ngai othe Marĩ na mbaara;
  Ithe wao no ũmwe Na nĩmendaine:
  ‘Kĩmakeni inyuothe A gwa Caitani!

4.Noguo nĩtũthiĩni O tũkenete,
  Naruo rwĩmbo rũrũ Tũkĩrũmatie,
  “Mũthamaki Kristũ nĩakumio mũno!”
  Noguo tene o na rĩu Tũmũinagĩra.

               Kũg 12:7-11
