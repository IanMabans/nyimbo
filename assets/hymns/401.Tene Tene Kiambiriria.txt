Tene, Tene Kĩambĩrĩria(309)
(The voice that breathed O’re Eden)

1.Tene, tene kĩambĩrĩria,
  Kĩhiko kĩa mbere,
  Ngai nĩarathimire
  Adamu na Hawa.

2.Rĩu no arathimaga
  Kĩhiko gĩtheru,
  Hĩndĩ ĩrĩa Akristiano
  Mekũhikithanio.

3.Ngai, ũka ũheane
  Mũhiki ũyũ rĩu,
  Ta ũrĩa waheire Adamu
  Mũka o tene ma.

4.O nawe, Roho Mũtheru,
  Ũũke hakuhĩ nao;
  Hĩndĩ ĩrĩa megũturia ndu
  Ũmarathimage.

5.ũmanyitithanie moko
  Jesũ Mũkũũri witũ;
  Ũmoimĩranithie ngoro
  Mendanage mũno.

6.Na ũmamenyagĩrĩre
  Tuĩka Mũmateithia,
  Thiritũ yao gũkũ thĩ
  Ũmĩgaacĩragie.

7.Na thuthainĩ makaambata,
  Mwathani aatũiyĩra,
  Maambatanie na Kanitha,
  Mũhiki wa Kristũ.


          Ef 5:31-33
