ũngĩmenyana Na Jesũ(256)
(When you know Jesus too)

1.ũngĩmenyana  na Jesũ,
  Mũnyitane moko nake,
  Wona ũtheri mwega ma;
  Ũngĩmenya Jesũ.

        Wamũmenya, wamũmenya,
        Mũkwendana nake kũna.
        Ũkĩone mũthenya mwerũ,
        Wamũmenya Jesũ ki.

2.ũkĩona gĩkeno gĩake,
  Kĩrathimo gĩake nakĩo;
  Wone kĩĩranĩro gĩake,
  Wamenya Jesũ ki.

3.ũkĩone tha ciake nyingĩ,
  Ũhoreri mĩaka yothe;
  Na gĩkuũ ndũgetigĩra,
  Wamenya Jesũ ki.

4.ũkĩmenye njĩra ciake
  Nocio njega cia kwĩhokwo;
  Ũheane ngumo yake,
  Wamenya Jesũ ki.


            Filip 3:7-16
