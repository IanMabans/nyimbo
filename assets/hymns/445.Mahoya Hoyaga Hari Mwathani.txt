Mahoya ‘Hoyaga Harĩ Mwathani(346)

1.Mahoya ‘hoyaga harĩ Mwathani,
  Mahoya ‘hoyaga harĩ Mwathani.

          “Ndonye harĩ we? Ndonye harĩ we?”
           Ũka, nĩngwetereire!”

2.Kĩhurũko kĩega harĩ Mwathani,
  Gĩkeno kĩa ngoro harĩ Mwathani.

3.Wendo ndũrĩ guoya harĩ Mwathani;
  Tiga mathangania wĩhoke Jesũ.

4.Kĩrĩro kĩnene kĩrĩ kwa ngoma;
  Mwago mũkonaga harĩ Mwathani.

5.”Nĩndakũiguĩra tha, ringa mũrango;
  Hurũka gĩtĩinĩ harĩ Mwathani.”

                      1 Joh 4:13-18
