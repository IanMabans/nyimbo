Riũa Nĩrĩthũire(339)
(Now the day is over)

1.Riũa nĩrĩthũire Na nĩ gũtuku;
  Gũkũgĩa nan duma, Njata nĩ nyumu;
  Mbũri o na Ng’ombe Irĩ kiugũinĩ;
  Andũ nĩmainũka, Nĩguo makome.

2.Arĩa anogu, Jesũ, ũmahe toro;
  Na niĩ tũma ngome Na kĩrathimo:
  Twana tũkĩrota tũrote nawe,
  Na andũ marĩ njĩra ũmatongorie.

3.Na arĩa arwaru, Manyamarũre;
  Mũndũ ũkwagana, ũmũgirie we:
  Tũma araika Manjikaragie,
  Mandigicagĩrie ũtukũ wothe.

4.Na rũcinĩ gwakĩa ũkanjũkĩria
  Itarĩ kaũũru Ga kuonwo nĩwe:
  Ithe nĩakumagio O na Mũrũ-we,
  Na Roho Mũtheru,Hingo ciothe!


                      Thim 3:24
