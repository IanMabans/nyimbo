Mwathani Jesũ Nĩagoka, Kirĩrĩria (131)
(Jesus Our Blessed Lord Will Come)

1.Mwathani Jesũ nĩagoka, Kirĩrĩria!!
  Mũhoyage hingo ciothe, Kirĩrĩria!!
  Rũgendoinĩ rũa Igũrũ,Ikaraga ũmwĩhokete:
  Nĩarĩtwaranaga nawe,Kirĩrĩria!!

2.Andũ anyu maagũthũũra, ũmĩrĩria!!
  Ũrĩa mũũru aakwĩhĩtĩra, ũmĩrĩria!!
  Ndakahota gũgũtoria;Mwathani Jesũ agũikairie
  Arorage ngoro yaku, ũmĩrĩria!!

3.Rĩrĩa ngoro ĩĩ na kĩeha, ũmĩrĩria!!
  Mathĩna maakũingĩhĩra,ũmĩrĩria!!
  Thĩ ĩno ndĩrĩ na ũhurũko;Thayũ witũ no he Jesũ,
  Ndarĩ hingo agatũtiga, ũmĩrĩria.

             Mat 25:13
