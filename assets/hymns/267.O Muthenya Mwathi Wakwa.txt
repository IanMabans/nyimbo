O Mũthenya, Mwathi Wakwa(209)
(Forth in thy Name O God)

1.O mũthenya, Mwathi wakwa,
  Nĩndĩtwaranaga nawe:
  Ngĩĩciria ,ngĩaria na ngĩĩka,
  Nowe wiki Ndĩĩmenyaga.

2.Wĩra ũrĩa ndanyĩire
  Na ũũgĩ mũkinyanĩru
  Ndĩũrutage ngenete
  Ndĩkĩrĩ maithoinĩ maku.

3.Ngũgũikaria mwena wakwa
  Wa ũrĩo, ũtuĩke Mũnduĩria,
  Ndungatage, ngĩathwo nĩwe
  Ũramati ngũtigĩre.

4.Ndĩĩhũthagĩra iheo
  Cia ũtaana waku mũingĩ,
  “Thiage na mbere nawe
  Ngĩhootanaga o wega.

5.’Hotithia kuoya icoki,
  Ndĩiguage ngĩkũhoya
  Njetereire o mũthenya
  Ũrĩa ũgacoka na riri.

          2 Tim 2:15
