Mũthenya Waku Mwathani, Nĩwathira(334)
(The day Thou Gavest, Lord is Ended)

1.Mũthenya waku Mwathani, nĩwathira,
  Nduma yoka ta ũrĩa wendete;
  Nĩtũkũinĩire rũcinĩ watũigua:
  Ũgooci waku ũrotũhurũkia.

2.Nĩ ngatho tondũ Kanitha ndũngĩkoma
  Ũtukũ wothe mũira gũkĩe;
  Nĩũikaraga wĩiguĩte kũndũ guothe
  Ũtekũhurũka hĩndĩ o yothe.

3.Gwakĩa mũthenya mwerũ wonekaga
  Mabũrũriinĩ mothe ma thĩ,
  Mahoya matirĩ hingo matithagio,
  Kana nyĩmbo cia ũgooci igathira,

4.ũthamaki waku ndũgathira o rĩ
  Ta marĩa ma thĩ marĩ nguthi:
  Ũcio nĩ mũrũmu, ningĩ waramaga
  Nginya ciũmbe ciothe igwathĩkĩre.

                    Luk 1:32,33
