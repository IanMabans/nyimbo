Ndĩ Rũgendoinĩ Ndĩrĩthiaga(229)
(walking in Sun Light)

1.Ndĩ rũgendoinĩ ndĩrĩthiaga
  Ndongoretio nĩ ũtheri wake,
  Ngĩtuĩkania irĩma na werũ
  Jesũ oigĩe,”Ndigagũtiga!”

           Riri wa Igũrũ, riri wa Igũrũ,
           Ũiyũrĩte  ngoro yakwa o ki;
           Haleluya! Ndĩ na gĩkeno
           Ngĩkũngũĩra Jesũ wakwa.

2.Nduma na Kĩeha ciandigicĩria,
  Itingĩhitha Mũhonokia;
  Nĩwe ũtheri, ndarĩ gatuma,
  Ndĩthiaga thiritũ nake.

3.Ndĩkenagĩra ũkengi wake,
  Ngĩĩheanaga nginye gwake;
  Nyinage nyĩmbo o ngĩgendaga
  Ũtheriinĩ wa wendo wake.

          Tham 13:21, 22
