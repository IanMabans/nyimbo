Nĩngũigua Mũgambo(42)
(I hear thy welcome voice)

1.Nĩngũigua mũgambo
  Wa Jesũ Mwathani;
  Nĩekũnjĩta harĩ we
  A’therie ngoro.

       Nĩngũũka, Jesũ,
       Njũke harĩ we:
       Theria ngoro yakwa rĩu
       Na thakame yaku.

2.Ngũũka ndĩ Mũmocu:
  Hinya waku ũ’he;
  Ngũũka ndĩ mwaganu mũno:
  Ũngirie kwagana.

3.Nĩtũgwĩtwo nĩ Jesũ
  Atũhonokie
  Atũhe gĩkeno gĩake
  Gĩa thĩ na Igũrũ.

4.Twakũgooca Mũkũũri,
  Tondũ wa thakame;
  Na tondũ wa wendo waku
  Twakũgooca mũno.

         Mar 10:46-52
