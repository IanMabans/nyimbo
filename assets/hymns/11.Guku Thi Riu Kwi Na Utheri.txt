Gũkũ thĩ rĩu kwĩ na ũtheri
(The Morning bright with rose light)

1.Gũkũ thĩ rĩu kwĩ na ũtheri;
  Kũrĩkĩtie gũkĩa:
  Nduma yothe nĩĩthirĩte,
  O na toro wakwa.

2.Ngai Baba, nĩũndarĩtie
  Ũtukũ uyu wothe:
  Ndũtigaga kũngitĩra
  Tondũ nĩũnyendete.

3.Na rĩu haha ndakũhoya
  Ũndindie ũmũthĩ:
  Mehia mothe ũnjohere;
  Na ũtuĩke mũndwari.

4.Tonya ngoroinĩ yakwa;
  Ndũgacoke kuuma:
  Njikarage hingo ciothe
  Ndĩ mwana waku ma.

               Thab 5:3, 8, 11
