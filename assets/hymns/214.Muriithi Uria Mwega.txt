Mũrĩithi ũrĩa Mwega
(Loving Shepherd of thy sheep)

1.Mũrĩithi ũrĩa mwega,
  Ũnjikarie wega:
  Ũngĩ’nyita na guoko,
  Tũtingĩtigithanio.

2.Mũhonokia mwende ma,
  Wakuire nĩ ũndũ witũ;
  Ũndeithie ngwendage,
  Na ngũcokerie ngaatho.

3.Nĩngwenda ngũkumie,
  Mĩthenya yakwa yothe,
  Ta andũ arĩa atheru
  Matũraga matuinĩ.

4.ũndongoragie niĩ,
  Ngwenda tũthiĩ nawe;
  O nginya tũgakinya
  Kũu gwaku Igũrũ.

             Joh 10:27-28
