ũka Kwĩ Jesũ Ndũkarege (270)
(Come to the Saviour Make No delay)

1.Ũka kwĩ Jesũ ndũkarege
  Ũka ũmũthĩ ũmwĩtĩkie
  Tondũ arĩ haha Jesũ Mwene
  Nake ekuuga “Njũ!”

       Tũgakena mũno mũno ma
       Twagirio gũcoka kwagana
       Tũtũũre nake Mũhonokia
       Tene o na tene

2.Ũka kwa Jesũ ndũkarege
  Reke ibuku rĩgũtaare
  Nĩrĩo rĩandĩke njĩra yake
  Nake ekuuga “Njũ!”

3.Ũka kwĩ Jesũ; ndũkarege;
  Igua mũgambo ũcio wake
  Rĩu ũmwĩtĩkie ũgĩkene
  Tondũ ekuuga “Njũ!”

4.Ũka kwĩ Jesũ ndũkarege
  Ngai nĩekwenda agũciare
  Nĩ ũndũ wa Jesũ akuohere
  Nake ekuuga “Njũ!”


              Ahib 4:6-8
