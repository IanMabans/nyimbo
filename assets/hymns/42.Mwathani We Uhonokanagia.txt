Mwathani we ũhonokanagia(34)

1.Mwathani we ũhonokanagia,
  Mwaki waku tũrehere;
  Tũikũrũkĩrie kĩheo gĩaku,
  Mwaki waku tũrehere;
  Tũhote kuona kũu Igũrũ,
  Tũikũrũkĩrie Roho mũtheru;
  Twĩkĩre hinya ngoroinĩ ciitũ;
  Mwaki waku tũrehere.

2.Ta ũrĩa Elija wamũcokeirie,
  Mwaki waku tũrehere;
  Tũthikĩrĩrie Mwathani wa tha,
  Mwaki waku tũrehere;
  Mehia macinwo ngoroinĩ ciitũ,
  Ũtũtherere na riri waku,
  Nĩtũkwenda wambĩrĩrie na ithuĩ;
  Mwaki waku tũrehere.

3.Tũkwenda mwaki rĩu twagũthaitha,
  Mwaki waku tũrehere;
  Tũtigane na mĩtugo mĩũru,
  Mwaki waku tũrehere:
  Twĩke maũndũ o ma ũthingu,
  Tũtigane na njĩra cia nduma,
  Mĩthiĩre iitũ ĩkũgoocithagie;
  Mwaki waku tũrehere.

4.Tũgũtungate twĩ na ũcamba,
  Mwaki waku tũrehere.
  Ehia metĩkĩre ũmahonokie,
  Mwaki waku tũrehere.
  Wĩtĩkĩre tũtuĩke igongona,
  Twenyihia magũrũinĩ maku;
  Mwathani,twakũhoya ũtũigue,
  Mwaki waku tũrehere.
