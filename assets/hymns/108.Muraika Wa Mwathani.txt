mũraika wa mwathani
(while shepherds watched their flocks by night)

1.Mũraika wa mwathani
  Na ũtheri mũnene
  Nĩokire kũrĩ arĩithi
  Nake akĩmera..

2.”Ndarehe ũhoro mwega
  Kwanyu, andũ a thĩ,
  Naguo nĩ wa kũmũkenia:
  Tigai kũmaka.

3.Mũhonokia Jesũ kristũ,
  Nĩwe ũciarĩtwo:
  Thiĩi Bethilehemu:
  Nĩmũrĩmuona kuo,

4.Gakenge kau kega ma
  Koimĩte matuinĩ
  Gatirĩ nguo ya mwago,
  Nake mũharatĩ.”

5.ũguo nĩguo aamerire,
  Na kahinda kangĩ
  Nĩhaaiyũrirũo nĩ aingĩ
  Mekũina hamwe,

6.”Kũgoocwo kũrogĩa kwa Ngai,
  Na wega guothe thĩ;
  Ngai arorathima andũ
  Rĩu na hingo ciothe.”

                 Luk 2:11
