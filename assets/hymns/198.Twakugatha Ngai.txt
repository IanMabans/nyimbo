Twakũgatha,Ngai(63)
(We Praise Thee O God)

1.Twakũgatha, Ngai,tondũ wa tha ciaku,
  Na thakame ya Jesũ Mũkũũri witũ.
       Haleluya! Twakũgatha:
       Haleluya! Amen,
       Haleluya! Twakũhoya,
       Tũrathime rĩu.

2.Tũrathime rĩu, ũtũiyũirie ngoro
  Wendani mũnene na wĩtĩkio .

3.Tũkũgatha Ngai tondũ Roho wake
  Nĩwe ũtũteithagia matukũmothe.

4.Jesũ nĩagoka; nĩtũkene mũno
  Na gĩkeno kĩnene na ũthethũko.


                 Tit 3:4-7
