Jesũ Nĩarĩhĩte Thiirĩ(99)
(Christ has for sin Atonement made)

1.Jesũ nĩarĩhĩte thiirĩ,
  Mũhonokia mũnene;
  Tũkũũrĩtwo nowe wiki,
  Mũhonokia mũnene.

                Mũhonokia mũnene
                Na mwega nĩ Jesũ,
                Mũhonokia mũnene
                Nĩ Mũrũ wa Ngai.

2.Nĩngũgatha wega mũno,
  Mũhonokia mũnene,
  Tondũ wa thakame yake,
  Mũhonokia mũnene.

3.Nĩatheretie ngoro yakwa,
  Mũhonokia mũnene,
  Ngaiguithanio na Ngai nĩwe,
  Mũhonokia mũnene.

4.Nĩngũthiĩ thiritũ na
  Mũhonokia mũnene
  Nĩngwĩtĩkia ndeto cia
  Mũhonokia mũnene.

5.Nĩandĩhĩire mathiirĩ,
  Mũhonokia mũnene,
  Na muoyo wake we mwene,
  Mũhonokia mũnene.

          1 Pet 1:18-21
