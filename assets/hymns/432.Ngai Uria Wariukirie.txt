Ngai ũrĩa Wariũkirie(332)
(Now may he who from the dead)

1.Ngai ũrĩa wariũkirie
  Mũrĩithi ũrĩa mũnene,
  Jesũ Kristũ Mũthamaki,
  Ũroikaria ngoro ciitũ.

2.Tũma tũtuĩke aagĩrĩru
  Ũndũinĩ wothe mwega,
  Tũkũhingagĩrie na ciĩko
  Kwenda gũtheru o gwaku.

3.Mũkũũri nĩ twakũgatha
  Tondũ waitire thakame
  Kĩrĩkanĩroinĩ kĩu kĩerũ:
  Ũrogoocagwo nginya tene.

       Ahib 13:20, 21
