Mũthenya ũmwe Jesũ Nĩagacoka(130)
(A little while Our Lord shall come)

1.Mũthenya ũmwe Jesũ nĩagacoka;
  Nĩakonwo nĩ andũ a gũkũ thĩ;
  Niagatwara andũ ake gwake,
  O arĩa othe mamwĩtĩkĩtie.

2.Mũthenya ũmwe Jesũ nĩagacoka:
  Ndeto ciake tũtingĩcitiga,
  No tũthiage na makinya make:
  Na nĩagatũhe gĩkeno kĩnene.

3.Hiũha, Jesũ, wee Mũhonokia witũ,
  Andũ aku ũmahonokie;
  Hiũha, Jesũ, wee Mũthamaki witũ,
  Ũthamakĩre andũ othe me thĩ.

           Kũg 1:7
