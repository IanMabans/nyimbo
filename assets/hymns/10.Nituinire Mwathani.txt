Nĩtũinĩre Mwathani (7)
(Let us with a gladsome mind)

1.Nĩtũinĩre Mwathani
  Amu nĩwe mũtugi.

        Tondũ ũtugi wake
        Nĩ wa tene na tene.

2.Nowe wiki mũnene:
  Nĩanĩrũo nĩ andũ othe!

3.Muone nowe Mwathani;
  Towe wakĩũmbire thĩ?

4.Indo ciothe ii muoyo
  Nĩwe mũtũũria wacio.

5.Ndarĩ hingo ataaiguire
  Andũ ake a tene.

6.Ithuĩ na ithuĩ twathĩna
  Nĩegũtũiguĩra tha ma.

7.Kwoguo nĩtũmũinĩre
  Na ngoro ikenete.

              Thab 136
