Jesũ Nĩoirire Thakame(98)
(Alas,and Did My Saviour Bleed)

1.Jesũ nĩoirire thakame;
  Nĩaakuire mũtĩinĩ;
  Nĩaarutire muoyo wake
  Nĩguo tũhonoke.

                Tũteithie Jesũ,tũkũhe
                Ngoro ciitũ o rĩu;
                Na rĩrĩa ũgacoka thĩ
                Tũtware Igũrũ.

2.Jesũ nĩambirũo mũtĩinĩ
  Tondũ wa wĩhia witũ;
  Gũtirĩ wendo mũnene
  Ta wendo wa Jesũ.

3.Jesũ ndekwenda thakame
  Na nyama cia mbũri;
  Nĩekwenda mũhera witũ
  Na wendo wa ngoro.

              1 Pet 2:24
