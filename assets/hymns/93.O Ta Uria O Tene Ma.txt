O ta ũrĩa o Tene ma(77)
(As With Gladness Men of Old)

1.O ta ũrĩa o tene ma
  Andũ monire njata;
  Nayo yamatongoria,
  Magĩkenio nĩyo ma;
  O na ithuĩ no taguo
  Tũtongorie, Mwathani.

2.O ta ũrĩa andũ ogĩ
  A njata maahiũhĩte
  Nĩguo makone Jesũ,
  Makamũturĩrie ndu;
  Ithuĩ noguo,Mwathani,
  Twĩhiũhaga harĩ we.

3.O ta ũrĩa maamũheire\
  Indo cia goro mũno;
  No ũguo o na ithuĩ
  Twĩrutaga na wendo,
  Iheo iria cia goro,
  Tondũ wĩ Mũthamaki.

4.Gwaku gũtirĩ bata
  Wa kwarĩrũo nĩ riũa;
  Jesũ nĩwe ũtheri
  O na gĩkeno gĩakuo;
  Kũu nĩtũinaga
  Tũgooce Mũthamaki.

5.Tũikaragie Mwathani
  Rĩrĩa tũrĩ gũkũ thĩ:
  Tũhootage Caitani,
  Twĩkage kwenda gwaku;
  Ningĩ,twarĩkia gũkua,
  Ũtũtware Igũrũ.

           Mat 2:1-12
