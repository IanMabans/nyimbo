Nĩmbatairio Nĩwe, Mwathani Mũtugi(294)
(I need thee every hour)

1.Nĩmbatairio nĩwe,
  Mwathani mũtugi;
  No mũgambo waku,
  Ũngĩhoreria.

      Nĩmbatairio nĩwe,
      Nĩmbatairio nĩwe;
      Ũndathime, Mũkũũri,
      Nĩngũũka harĩ we.

2.Nĩmbatairio nĩwe,
  Njikaria hakuhĩ;
  Magerio mahootagwo,
  O rĩrĩa ũrĩ ho.

3.Nĩmbatairio nĩwe,
  O mahinda mothe,
  Makĩeha na ruo,
  Kana magĩkeno.

4.Nĩmbatairio nĩwe,
  o we Mũtheru,
  Ũnyamũre rĩu,
  We Mũrũ wa Ngai.

           2 Tim 4:16-18
