Mũtikaneke ũũru
(Do no Sinful Action)

1.Mũtikaneke ũũru
  Kana mũrakare:
  Mũrĩ twana twa Jesũ;
  Mũrĩ a Mũnene.

2.Jesũ nĩ mũhoreri;
  Nowe mũthingu ma:
  Ningĩ na twana twake,
  No tũgĩthinge ma.

3.Kũrĩ na ngoma njũru
  Ĩmũikarĩtie,
  Nayo nĩĩmũcemaga
  Ĩkamwaganie.

4.Mũtikanathwo nĩyo,
  Ndĩkanamũhenie;
  Mũmĩregage hinya
  Mũtikahoteke.

5.Mũrĩ a Jesũ Kristũ
  Ũrĩa mũthingu ma;
  Ningĩ mwĩ twana twake
  No mũgĩthinge ma.

          1 Kor 10:13
