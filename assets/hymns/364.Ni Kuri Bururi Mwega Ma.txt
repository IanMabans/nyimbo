Nĩ Kũrĩ Bũrũri Mwega Ma(281)
(There’s a land that is fairer Than Day)

1.Nĩ kũrĩ bũrũri mwega ma,
  Na tũngĩtĩkia twawona
  Amu Ngai nĩatũire o kuo,
  Atũthondekagĩra mũciĩ.

        Igũrũ kwa Jesũ,
        Nĩtũgacemania o kuo;
        Igũrũ, kwa Jesũ,
        Nĩtũgacemania o kuo.

2.Nĩtũkaina nyĩmbo njega,
  Iria cia andũ arĩa atheru,
  O namo maroho maitũ
  Matikaigua ruo rĩngĩ.

3.Nĩtũgathage Ithe witũ,
  Tũmũcokagĩrie mũhera,
  Tondũ nĩ wendo wake mwene
  Ũtũmaga tũkenagio.



          Ahib 12:22-24
