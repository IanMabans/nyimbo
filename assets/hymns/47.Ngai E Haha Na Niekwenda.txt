Ngai E Haha na Nĩekwenda(38)
(God is here and that to bless us)

1.Ngai e haha na nĩekwenda
  Gũtũrathima ithuothe;
  Andũ aitũ nĩtũhoe
  Tũheo Roho wake.

        Tũrathime twakũhoya,
        Tuurĩrie Kĩrathimo;
        Mwathani tũgwetereire
        Ũtũhe hinya mwerũ.

2.Ngai e haha hamwe na ithuĩ
  Handũ haha hatheru,
  Andũ othe nĩtũhoe
  Atherie ngoro ciitũ.

3.Ngai e haha na nĩekwenda
  Tũmũhe ngoro ciitũ,
  Atũiyũrie wendo wake
  Na kĩrathimo gĩake.

4.Mwathani rĩu ũtũigue,
  Nĩtwenyihia harĩwe,
  Tũikũrũkĩrie irathimo,
  Tũiyũrĩrĩrũo ngoro.

         Thab 24:3-6
