Muoyo Wakwa Ndakũhe ũtuĩke(191)
(Take My Life and Let it Be)

1.Muoyo wakwa ndakũhe
  Ũtuĩke igongona;
  Mahinda makwa mothe
  Oya makũgoocithagie.

2.Moko namo ndakũhe
  Mekage kwenda gwaku;
  O na magũrũ makwa
  Makĩrekagio nĩwe.

3.Mũgambo wakwa naguo
  Ũiyũkie ngũkumagie;
  Iromo ciakwa nacio
  Ciaragie Kiugo gĩaku.

4.Ndakũhe indo ciakwa
  Iria ciothe ndĩ nacio;
  Meciria namo oya,
  Ũhũthĩre ũrĩa ũkwenda.

5.Wendi wakwa ndakũhe
  Ũtuĩke waku wiki;
  Ngoro yakwa kĩmĩoe
  Ũmĩthamakagĩre.

6.Wendo wakwa ndakũhe
  Uumĩte ngoro thĩinĩ;
  Ũnjiyũkie ndĩ wothe;
  Ndakwĩhe nginya tene.

              Rom 12:1
