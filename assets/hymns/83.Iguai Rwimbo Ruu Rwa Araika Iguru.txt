Iguai Rwĩmbo Rũu Rwa Araika Igũrũ(65)
(Herk the Herald Angels Sing)

1.Iguai rwĩmbo rũu rwa araika Igũrũ,
  Makĩinĩra Jesũ Kristo makĩmũgooca mũno;
  Nĩwe ũrĩa waciarĩirũo itũũra rĩa Bethilehemu,
  Nĩtũgathe Mwathani,Mũthamaki mũciare.

2.Kristũ ũrĩa ũkumagio nĩ araika Igũrũ,
  Nĩwe Mũthamaki witũ o na wa andũ othe;
  Nĩatũũrire thĩ ĩno e na mwĩrĩ o ta witũ,
  Nĩtũgathe Mwathani,Mũthamaki mũciare.

3.Twakũgatha mũno Jesũ,Mũthamaki wa thayũ,
  Tondũ nĩũtũheaga ũhoreri na muoyo,
  Nĩwaregire ũnene o ta mũndũ mũhoreri;
  Rĩu wĩ mũnene witũ,o na ithuĩ twĩ aku.

4.Nĩtũgũkũhoya ũtũhe Roho waku Mũtheru
  Aikarage ngoroinĩ tũtoragie Caitani;
  Ũtũme tũtuĩke rĩu andũ aku arĩa atheru.
  Nĩguo andũ monage atĩ tũrĩ a Jesũ.

                  Luk 2
