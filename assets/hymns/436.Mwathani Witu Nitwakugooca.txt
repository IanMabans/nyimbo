Mwathani Witũ, Nĩtwakũgooca(336)
(Saviour again to thy dear name)

1.Mwathani witũ, nĩtwakũgooca
  Tondũ wa rĩĩtwa rĩaku rĩtheru;
  Tũgweterera ũtũrathime,
  Nĩguo tũinũke turĩ na thayũ.

2.Tũhe thayũ ngoroinĩ ciitũ,
  Tũkorũo hamwe nawe ũtukũ:
  Ũgitĩre arĩa othe aku
  Magũthaithe haha ũmũthĩ:

3.ũtũhe thayũ ũtukũ wothe,
  Ndumainĩ tũthiĩ na ũtheri;
  Ũtũikarie twĩ andũ aku,
  Ũũru wothe ũtwehererie;

4.ũtũhe thayũ mĩthenya yothe,
  Tũteithagio magerioinĩ;
  Nĩguo ihinda rĩa gĩkuũ giitũ
  Tũtwarũo kũrĩ arĩa atheru.

             Kĩam 32:24-30
