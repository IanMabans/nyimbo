Njiguithagia Mũgambo Waku(312)
(Lord speak to me that I may speak)

1.Njiguithagia mũgambo waku, Jesũ,
  Nĩguo ‘hotage kwaragia tawe;
  ‘Heaga wendo waku wa gũcaria
  Mbũri iria ciũrĩte kũraya.

2.Jesũ, ndeithia ‘hote gũtongoragia
  Andũ  arĩa morũraga werũinĩ;
  Na ũ’hũnagie na maana me mũrĩo
  Nĩguo arĩa ahũtu nao mahũne.

3.We ihiga rĩakwa, njĩkĩra hinya,
  Ndũgamia wega ndikae kũgwa;
  No ndambũrũkie guoko na wendani
  ‘Hatũrage arĩa mahatĩte.

4.Jesũ, nduta ‘hote kũrutanaga
  Maũndũ manene ũnguũrĩirie;
  Ũtũme kiugo gĩaku gĩkinyage
  Hau hahitharu ngoroinĩ cia andũ.

5.Njiyũrĩrĩria na ũtugi waku,
  Nayo ngoro yakwa yembembane:
  Nĩ getha ng’anage ũgooci waku
  Na ngĩonanaga ũgooci waku.

6.Jesũ, ‘hũthagĩra ũrĩa ũkwenda,
  Handũ o hothe, mahinda mothe;
  Nginya ngona ũthiũ waku wĩ riri
  Ngahurũka gĩkenoinĩ gĩaku.

                 Isa 50:4
