ũndongorie, Mwathani Jesũ(238)
(Guide Me, o thou great Jehovah)

1.ũndongorie, Mwathani Jesũ,
  Ndĩ mũgendi werũinĩ;
  Ndĩ mũhũthũ, Mwene-Hinya,
  ‘Nyita na guoko gwaku:
  Wee Mũgate wa Igũrũ,
  Hũnia ngoro yakwa.

2.Gĩthima kĩa maĩ ma muoyo,
  Therũka thĩinĩ wakwa;
  Narĩo itu rĩa ũtheri
  Nĩgwenda rĩndongorie,
  Norĩo rĩngĩnyonia njĩra
  Rĩrĩa ngũthiĩ ũtukũ.

3.Mwathani nĩarĩndwaraga
  Nginya ndĩkie rũgendo;
  Rũũĩ rwa gĩkuũ rũtingĩ’makia,
  Akaningia o wega,
  Ndonye bũrũri wa Igũrũ
  Ta ũrĩa eranĩire.

              1 Kor 10:1-4
