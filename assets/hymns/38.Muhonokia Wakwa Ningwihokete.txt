Mũhonokia wakwa, Nĩngwĩhokete(29)
(My faith looks up to thee)

1.Mũhonokia wakwa,nĩngwĩhokete ma,Mũndangĩri;
  Njigua,ngĩkũhoya, theria mehia makwa,
  Nduĩke kuuma ũmũthĩ waku kũna.

2.Reke wega waku ũnjĩkĩre hinya ngoroinĩ;
  Nĩwe wanguĩrĩire;tũma wendo waku
  Ũrĩrĩmbũkage thĩinĩ wakwa.

3.Nduma ya gũkũ thĩ,nĩ ndumanu mũno,Ngai wakwa;
  Ndongoria,Mwathani,ngũrũmagĩrĩre,
  Ndikanatigane nawe o rĩ.

4.Nĩguo ndute wĩra,ũrĩa ũ’heete thĩ,ngũgoocithie;
  Rĩrĩa ngaũrĩkia,ũndware Igũrũ,
  Tũtũũranie nawe nginya tene.

                     Isa 40:28-31
