Awa, Nĩtwongana haha rĩu
(Father, again in jesus' Name we meet)

1. Awa, nĩtwongana haha rĩu,
   Nĩ ũndũ wa rĩĩtwa rĩega rĩa Jesũ;
   Tũgagũthaithe ũtũrathime.

2. Tũkwenda gũkũgatha gũkũ  rĩu,
   Tondũ wa gũikario nĩwe wega;
   Nowe wiki ũtũtongoragia,
   na tha, ũgatũrigicĩria.

3. Tũrĩ athĩni o na ahĩnju,
   Hĩndĩ ciothe nĩtwĩkaga ũru;
   Tũkũigua,Awa,mũgambo waku,
   Tũkwenda rĩu gũgũcokerera.

4. Kwondũ wa rĩĩtwa rĩaku rĩtheru,
   Kwondũ wa tha, na wendo wa jesũ,
   Kwondũ wathakame yaitirũo,
   Twagũthaitha ũtwĩtĩkĩre rĩu.

                      Heb 7:19
