Gwĩ Karĩma Ge Kũraya(96)
(There is a green hill)

1.Gwĩ karĩma ge kũraya
  Hau nja ya itũũra;
  Nĩho Mwathani o tene
  Aambĩirũo mũtĩinĩ.

2.Tũtingĩhota Kũmenya
  Ũrĩa aathĩnire;
  Nĩtũũĩ nĩ ũndũ witũ
  Aanyamarĩkire.

3.Tondũ wa mawĩhia maitũ
  Nĩaatũkuĩrĩire,
  Atũkũũre na thakame
  Nĩguo thonoke.

4.Gũtirĩ ũngĩ tiga we
  Wa gũtũrĩhĩra;
  Nowe wiki wahotire
  Tondũ wa kwagĩra.

5.Nĩatwendete mũno mũno;
  Na ithuĩ nĩtũmwende,
  Tũmwĩtĩkĩre ti itherũ,
  Tũtuĩke ake we.

          Ahib 13:12
