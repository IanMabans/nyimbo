Ngũũka Ndige Nduma Na Kĩeha(195)
(Out of My Bondage)

1.Ngũũka ndige nduma na kĩeha,
  Ngũũka o rĩu, gwaku Jesũ;
  Ũnjohore nyume ũkomboinĩ,
  Ngũũka gwaku Jesũ.
  Ndĩ mũrũaru, ngũũka ũ’honie,
  Ũkĩ’ninĩre ũkĩa wakwa
  Ndige mehia njũke harĩ we,
  Ngũũka gwaku Jesũ.

2.ũremi wakwa na ũitangi
  Nĩngũitiga, njũke gwaku;
  Ndongoragio nĩ mũtharaba,
  Ngũũka gwaku Jesũ.
  Ngũtiga wĩhia, ũ’he thayũ,
  Ũ’hurũkie ndige gwĩtanga,
  ‘Ninĩra ruo, ngũinagĩre;
  Ngũũka gwaku Jesũ.

3.Ngũtiga ngũrũ na mwĩtĩo,
  Ngũũka o rĩu,gwaku Jesũ;
  Ũnjathage na ũngitĩre;
  Ngũũka gwaku Jesũ.
  Ndige kwĩigania, ngwĩhoke,
  Ndige kĩeha, ndĩkenagĩre,
  Nyũmbũkage ngorĩra thĩna
  Ngũũka gwaku Jesũ.

4.Ngũtiga guoya na ihoru,
  Ngũũka o rĩu,gwaku Jesũ
  Ũnjarĩre na ũtheri waku
  Ngũũka gwaku Jesũ.
  Ndige kwanangĩkaga ndĩ thĩ
  Ndonye thayũinĩ kiugũ gĩaku
  Ndĩroragĩre riri waku
  Ngũũka gwaku Jesũ.


              Joh 8:34-36
