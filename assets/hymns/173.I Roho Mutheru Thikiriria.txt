ĩ, Roho Mũtheru,Thikĩrĩria(141)
(Come Holy Ghost our Source Inspire)

1.ĩ,Roho Mũtheru,thikĩrĩria;
  Ũka o haha,twagũthaitha ma;
  Ũtũikũrũkĩre na hinya waku:
  Roho Mũtheru,tũiyũre ngoro.

2.Na ũũke ta ũtheri wa gũtuonia
  Ũrimũ witũ o na wĩhia;
  Na nĩguo tũhote gũthiĩ wega
  Ũtuonie njĩra ta twĩ na twana.

3.Na ũũke ta mwaki nĩguo ũcine
  Ũũru ũrĩa ũtũthahĩtie;
  Tũtungatagĩre Mũkũũri witũ
  Ngoro ciitũ ihorohetio.

4.Na ũũke ta mbura ya kũmeria
  Ciĩko nyingĩ cia gũgũkenia,
  Tũhaane mũgũnda mũnoru mũno
  Ũtangĩaga kuuma irio.

5.Na ũũke ningĩ ta rũhuho o huu!
  Ta ũrĩa wokire,ningĩ ũũke  rĩu;
  Kĩrĩndĩ gĩkaigue mĩciĩinĩ yao
  Makũiyũkie ngoroinĩ ciao.

6.ĩ,Roho Mũtheru, thikĩrĩria;
  Ũka thĩ yothe, twagũthaitha ma;
  Ũtũikũrũkĩre na ũtugi waku:
  Roho Mũtheru,  no gĩũke o rĩu.

                    Atũm 2:1-4
