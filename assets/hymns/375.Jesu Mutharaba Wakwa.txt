Jesũ Mũtharaba Wakwa(287)
(Jesus I my Cross have taken)

1.Jesu, mũtharaba wakwa
  Rĩu nĩndeigĩrĩra
  Hikĩre Mwathani wakwa
  O na angĩ mamũtiga

          Nĩngũkũrũmĩrĩra we
          We wanguĩrĩire
          Kaba arata mandige
          Ngũrũmagĩrĩre we

2.O na mangĩnyamaria atĩa
  Ndĩũragĩra harĩ we
  Mathĩna mothe mangĩũka
  Nĩndĩmomagĩrĩria

3.Ndirĩ na thuti ya indo
  Ici cia gũkũ thĩ ĩno
  Niĩ ndĩ gĩtonga kũna
  Ndĩna igai kwa Ngai

4.Mũrata, nawe hanyũka
  Na wĩhumbe wĩtĩkio
  Nĩguo ũgakinya igũrũ
  Gĩkeno-inĩ kwa Ngai

           Mat 10:37-39
