Jesũ Tũrathime(25)
(Jesus stand Among Us)

1.Jesũ tũrathime
  Tũkĩhoya rĩu,
  Tũma ihinda rĩrĩ
  Rĩtuĩke itheru,

2.Tũhe Roho waku
  Ngoroinĩ ciitũ;
  Atũingatagĩre
  Watho wa Caitani.

3.Tũheage hinya
  Tũgĩthiĩ na njĩra,
  ĩrĩa ĩthiaga
  Gwaku kũrĩa kwega.

4.Tũhe ũhoreri
  Ngoroinĩ ciitũ;
  Tũma andũ mone
  Tũrĩ ciana ciaku.

        Luk 24:36
