ĩ Roho Mũtheru, Tũigue Tũkĩina(139)
(Holy Spirit Hear us)

1.ĩ Roho Mũtheru, tũigue tũkĩina,
  Tũteithie tũgooce Ngai wa wendo.

2.Na ũtũtaarage tũgĩturia ndu,
  Ũũke ũtũrute kũhoya wega.

3.ĩ Roho Mũtheru, ũtũtherere
  Na Ibuku rĩa Ngai; tũtuganage.

4.Ningĩ ũtwĩkĩre wĩnyihia wa ma;
  Tũhaane ta Jesũ, tũtuganage.

5.ĩ Roho Mũtheru, tũkenagĩre
  Mawĩra ma hinya ma kũguna andũ.

6.Naguo hinya waku ũtũhotithie
  Tũtoragie ũũru twende ũthingu.

                      Rom 8:26,27
