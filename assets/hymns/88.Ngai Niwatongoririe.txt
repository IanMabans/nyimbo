Ngai Nĩwatongoririe(76)
(Oh Thou Who By Star)

1.Ngai nĩwatongoririe
  Andũ na njata ĩo,
  Magĩkora Jesũ witũ
  Mũharatĩinĩ.

2.Rĩu ndũtongoragia andũ
  Na njata ta tene;
  Nĩ Roho wak Mũtheru
  Ũtũtongoragia.

3.Ningĩ nĩũmarathimaga
  Mũthenya na ũtukũ.
  Mũhonokia,twagũthaitha
  Ũtũtherie ngoro.

4.Ithuĩ nĩtũkwenda mũno
  Twambate Igũrũ,
  Tuonane na Mũhonokia
  Na tũmũgoocage.

                Mat 2
