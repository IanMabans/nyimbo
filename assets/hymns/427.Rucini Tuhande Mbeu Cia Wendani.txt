Rũcinĩ Tũhande Mbeũ Cia Wendani(212)
(Sowing in the Morning)

1. Rũcinĩ tũhande, mbeũ cia wendani
  Na mĩaraho, o na hwai-inĩ narĩo
  Jesũ nĩetereire, rĩrĩa tũkagetha
  Tũkamũrehere, magetha maitũ

         Twarehe manja, twarehe manja
         Tũgakena mũno twamũrehere
         Twarehe manja, twarehe manja
         Tũgakena mũno twamũrehere

2.Tũhande riũa-inĩ, o na ciĩruru-inĩ
  O na kũrĩ matu, kana kwĩ heho
  Twarĩkia kũgetha, na wĩra wathira
  Tũkamũrehere magetha maitũ

3.Handĩrai Jesũ, na mũmũrĩrĩre
  O na muona thĩna, kana kĩeha kaĩngĩ
  Twarĩkia kũgetha, nĩagatũiyũkia
  Tũkĩmũrehere magetha maitũ.

              Thab 126
