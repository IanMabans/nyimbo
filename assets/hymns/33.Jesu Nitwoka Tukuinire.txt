Jesũ Nĩtwoka Tũkũinĩre(24)
(Lord a little  band and lowly)

1.Jesũ nĩtwoka tũkũinĩre
  Twĩ gakundi kanini;
  Na tondĩ nowe Mũnene
  Tũthikĩrĩrie rĩu;
  Nawe nĩũtĩiguaga;
  Andũ othe nĩ aku!
  Rĩu twoka gũkũhoya
  Twĩtĩkĩre,mwathani.

2.ũtwĩkĩre wendo ngoro
  Nĩguo tũkwendage ma;
  Tũthũũrithie ũũru wothe;
  Mehia na ũremi witũ,
  Tuohere na tha, Jesũ,
  Tuonie njĩra ya gwaku,
  Mũtũteithia wa Igũrũ,
  ũguo nĩguo twahoya.

            Math 18:20
