Rurumũkia Wĩra Waku(198)
(Revive Thy Work, Oh Lord!)

1.Rurumũkia wĩra waku,
  Ngai, wĩonithanie:
  Kaĩrĩria na hinya ngoro ciothe,
  Andũ aku maigue.

         Tũriũkie Mwathani!
         Twakũinamĩrĩra:
         Ũtũikũrũkĩre, Mwathani:
         Ũka ũtũrathime!

2.Rurumũkia wĩra waku!
  Ngai, wĩgocithagie:
  Ngoro ciitũ ciothe ciakanage
  Wendo waku Jesũ.

3.Rurumũkia wĩra waku,
  Rathima ndeto ciaku;
  Nĩguo ũhoro wa ma na wa ũthingu
  Ũiguo na wĩtĩkio.

4.Riũkia wĩra waku, Ngai,
  Tũitĩrĩrie Roho;
  Ũnene ũtuĩke waku wiki,
  Na ithuĩ tũrathimwo.


             Hab 3:2
