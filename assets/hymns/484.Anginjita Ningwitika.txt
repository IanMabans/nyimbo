Angĩnjĩta Nĩngwĩtĩka

1.Angĩnjĩta nĩngwĩtĩka,
  Angĩnjĩta nĩngwĩtĩka,
  Nĩngwĩtĩka Mwathani wakwa.

              Ndĩtungatagĩra Mwathani,
              Ndĩtungatagĩra Mwathani wakwa,
              Ndĩtungatagĩra Mwathani,
              Ndĩtungatagĩra Mwathani wakwa.

2.Angĩndũma nĩngũthiĩ,
  Angĩndũma nĩngũthiĩ,
  Nĩngũthiĩ kũrĩa We ekũndũma.

3.Ingĩgwa We no anjoe,
  Ingĩgwa We no anjoe,
  Mwathani wakwa no anjoe.
