Nĩwe ũhĩtĩtie Njĩra(267)
(My Son, Me Thy Heart)

1.Nĩwe, ũhĩtĩtie njĩra,
  Ũcangĩte mehiainĩ,
  Nĩũkwarĩrio nĩ Thoguo Ngai:
  Mũriũ, ‘he ngoro rĩu.
        Mũriũ, mũriũ, ‘he ngoro rĩu.
        Igua mũgambo wa Thoguo,
        Mũhe ngoro yaku rĩu.

2.Mũgambo ũcio nĩnjũĩ
  Nĩguo wanjĩtaga tene,
  Na rĩu nĩguo ũrakwĩra:
  Mũriũ, ‘he ngoro rĩu.

3.Wendo ũtangĩĩranĩka
  Nĩ ũyũ wa Ngai Mũnene,
  Na nĩkĩo aragũthaitha:
  Mũriũ, ‘he ngoro rĩu.

4.Ngai Baba, mũgambo waku
  Nĩũnjĩtaga na wendo.
  Ũmũthĩ nĩngwenda gũũka
  Ngũhe ngoro yakwa:
                  Awa, nyamũkĩra o rĩu,
                  Nĩndaigua mũgambo waku
                  Na nĩndakũhe ngoro.

                    Thim 23:26
