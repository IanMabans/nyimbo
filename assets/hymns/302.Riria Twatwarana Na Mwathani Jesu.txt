Rĩrĩa Twatwarana Na Mwathani Jesũ
(When we walk with the Lord)

1.Rĩrĩa twatwarana na Mwathani Jesũ,
  Tũmũrĩkĩirwo njĩra nĩwe;
  Rĩrĩa twathĩkĩra uuge wake noguo,
  Nake arĩĩtwaranaga na ithuĩ.

          Tũmwĩhoke, na tũmwathĩkĩre,
          Tondũ njĩra no ĩmwe,
          Gwĩtĩkia Mwathani.

2.Nduma yatumana, nganja, guiya cioka,
  Tũmĩrĩrie twĩ rũgendoinĩ;
  Jesũ e hamwe na ithuĩ,no tũkahotana,
  Tũngĩathĩka na tũmwĩhoke.

3.Nĩ hinya kũmenya, wendo wa Mwathani,
  O kinya tũmenyithio nĩwe,
  Gĩkeno kĩnene kĩrĩa twĩheagwo;
  Twamwĩhoka ningĩ twathĩka.

4.Twatwarana nake, rũgendoinĩ rũrũ,
  Tũnyitanĩte moko nake;
  Twatũmwo tũgathiĩ tũtegwĩtigĩra,
  No kũmwĩhoka na gwathĩka.

               Rom 16:26
