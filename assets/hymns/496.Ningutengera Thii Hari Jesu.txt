Nĩngũteng’era Thiĩ Harĩ Jesũ
(I have decided to follow Jesus)

1.Nĩngũteng’era thiĩ harĩ Jesũ,
  Nĩngũteng’era thiĩ harĩ Jesũ,
  Nĩngũteng’era thiĩ harĩ Jesũ,
  Na ndingĩcoka na thutha,
  Na ndingĩcoka na thutha.

2.Jesũ witũ nĩatwĩraga,
  Jesũ witũ nĩatwĩraga,
  Jesũ witũ nĩatwĩraga,
  Rehei ehia kũrĩ niĩ,
  Reheni ehia kũrĩ niĩ.

3.Nĩokire gũkũ thĩ ĩno,
  Nĩokire gũkũ thĩ ĩno,
  Nĩokire gũkũ thĩ ĩno,
  Na agĩkua nĩũndũ witũ,
  Na agĩkua nĩũndũ witũ.

4.Twamwĩhoka Jesũ we wiki,
  Twamwĩhoka Jesũ we wiki,
  Twamwĩhoka Jesũ we wiki,
  Nĩtũgakinya Igũrũ,
  Nĩtũgakinya Igũrũ.

5.Tũgakenaga twakinya Igũrũ,
  Tũgakenaga twakinya Igũrũ,
  Tũgakenaga twakinya Igũrũ,
  Tũkĩgooca Mũthamaki,
  Tũkĩgooca Mũthamaki.

              2 Tim 2:4
