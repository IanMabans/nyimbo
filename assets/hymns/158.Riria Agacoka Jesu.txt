Rĩrĩa Agacoka Jesũ(134)
(Zion’s King shall Reign Victorious)

1.Rĩrĩa agacoka Jesũ
  Oke na hinya wothe,
  Nĩagatuĩka Mũthamaki
  Nginya tene na tene.

2.Hĩndĩ ĩo andũ othe
  Nĩmakona ũtheri,
  Na mbere yake ũtukũ
  Ũgatuĩka mũthenya.

3.O na arĩa maamũthũũrire
  Na makĩũraga Jesũ,
  Magathĩna mũno ngoro
  Mona nowe Mũnene.

4.Ngai Mwene-hinya-wothe,
  Ũtũgucie kũrĩ we,
  Andũ ndũrĩrĩ ciothe
  Matuĩke andũ aku.

                    Mat 24:30
