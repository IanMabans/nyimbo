Ariũ A Ithe Wĩtũ, Inyuĩ Mũhonoketio(187)

1.Ariũ a Ithe witũ, inyuĩ mũhonoketio,
  Mwĩciragie mũno kĩrĩa mwĩtĩirũo.

2.Mũthenya nĩũgakinya rĩrĩa tũkorio ngoro
  Cia andũ othe aitũ, o na itũũra.

3.Rekei twĩheane tũhunjie ũhonokio,
  Roho atũhũthĩre, ngoro ihonio.

4.Arĩa matakũũrĩtwo maiyũire matũũrainĩ
  Makahonokio atĩa twagĩa na guoya?

5.Mwathani nĩerutire, agĩgĩĩcuma riri,
  Nĩguo atũhonokie; tũtuĩke njamba!

6.Nĩtũoe mũtharaba wa ũrĩa watũgũrire
  Na waitirũo thakame; tũmuume thutha.

7.Ithuĩ twendete Jesũ,arĩa tũhonoketio,
  Nĩtwĩheanei rĩu aingĩ makũũrũo.

                     Atũm 20:24-28
