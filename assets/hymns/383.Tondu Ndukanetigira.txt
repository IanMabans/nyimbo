Tondũ Ndũkanetigĩra (296)
(In token that thou shalt not fear)

1.Tondũ ndũkanetigĩra
  Kuumbũra Jesũ,
  Twagwĩkĩra mũtharaba
  Ũtuĩke nĩ rũũri.

2.Nĩ ũndũ wee ndũrĩtigaga
  Gũtwarana nake,
  Amũkĩra ngumo yake
  O na thoni ciake.

3.Nĩ ũndũ wee ndũrĩtigaga
  Gũtuĩka mwĩtĩkia
  Na kũrũĩra rĩĩtwa rĩake,
  Ndũkanetigĩre.

4.Rĩu twakũiga harĩ we
  Na twakwĩra o ma,
  “Wĩ nguruĩ ya Mũtharaba
  Na thũmbĩ no yaku.”

              Rom 10:9, 10
