ũkani, Inyuĩ Ehia(273)
(come every soul by sin oppressed)

1.ũkani, inyuĩ ehia,
  Mũũke kwĩ Mwathani;
  Muone ũhoreri wake
  Endete kũmũhe.

        Mwĩtĩkiei, mwĩtĩkie, mwĩtĩkie  rĩu;
        Nĩ Mũkũũri, nĩ Mũkũũri, wanyu.

2.Aatuĩke igongona
  Rĩa kũnina mehia,
  Akĩĩruta muoyo wake
  Nĩguo tũhonoke.

3.Njĩra ĩrĩa ya Mwathani
  Nĩ ya ũhoreri;
  Gera njĩra yake o rĩu
  Ũkĩone gĩkeno.

4.Noguo ithuĩ ĩthuothe
  Nĩtũthiĩi hamwe,
  Tũtũũranie nake Igũrũ
  Tene o na tene.

                Mar 2:17
