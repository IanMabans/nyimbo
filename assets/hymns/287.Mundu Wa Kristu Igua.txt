Mũndũ Wa Kristũ Igua (225)
(Christian seek Not yet Repose)

1.Mũndũ wa Kristũ, igua,
  Rĩu tiga gũcũnga;
  Thũ ciaku nĩ nyingĩ ma,
  “Wĩhũge.”

2.Andũ othe a nduma
  Nĩmegũkũũnganĩra,
  Makĩenda gũkũhoota,
  “ũhoe.”

3.Na wĩohe narua,
  Indo ciothe cia mbaara;
  Ũtukũ na mũthenya,
  “Wĩhũge.”

4.Ngai nĩaahotithirie
  Andũ ake a tene;
  Igua rĩu ciugo ciske,
  “ũhoe.”

5.Hoya Ngai akũhe
  Hinya wake mũnene,
  Ũtoragie Caitani,
  “Wĩhũge.”

             Mat 26:40,41
