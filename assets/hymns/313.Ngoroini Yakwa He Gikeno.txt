Ngoroinĩ Yakwa He Gĩkeno(246)
(There’s within my heart a melody)

1.Ngoroinĩ yakwa he gĩkeno
  Tondũ Jesũ nĩanjĩĩraga,
  “Tiga gwĩtigĩra, ndĩ nawe
  Maũndũinĩ mothe.

        Jesũ, Jesũ, Jesũ,
        Rĩĩtwa rĩega ma;
        Aiganagia ngoro yakwa,
        Na akangenia hĩndĩ ciothe.

2.Tene ndarĩ mwĩhia mũno ma;
  Mehia maanjiyũire ngoro;
  Jesũ nĩaanguĩrĩire ngoro;
  Agĩ’theria ngoro ki.

3.Mahinda mamwe nĩ’thĩnagio,
  Njĩra ĩĩ nan duma nene;
  Jesũ ndangĩndiga, arĩ ho,
  Anyonagie ũtheri.

4.Jesũ nĩegũcoka narua,
  Andware gwake Igũrũ,
  Ndũũranagie nake kwa Ngai
  Ũthamakiinĩ wake.


                 Luk 12:31, 32
