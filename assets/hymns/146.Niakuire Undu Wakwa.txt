Nĩakuire ũndũ wakwa
(wounded For Me)

1.Nĩakuire ũndũ wakwa,
  Agĩkua hau mũtharabainĩ.
  Mehia makwa nĩanguĩrĩire.
  Tondũ Jesũ nĩanguĩrĩire.

2.Ithenya rĩakwa, Ithenya rĩakwa,
  Jesũ nĩakuire ithenya rĩakwa.
  Nĩndekeirũo mehia mothe,
  Tondũ Jesũ nĩanguĩrĩire.

3.ũndũ wakwa, ũndũ wakwa ,
  Jesũ nĩahoyaga ũndũ wakwa,
  Atũire a’thaithanagĩra
  Na mũcayo ũtangĩgweteka.

4.Handũ hakwa,handũ hakwa,
  Nĩ’heetwo nyũmbainĩ ya Baba,
  Jesũ nĩagoka kũndwara Igũrũ,
  Tũikare mũciĩ wake mũtheru.

                  Isa 53:5
