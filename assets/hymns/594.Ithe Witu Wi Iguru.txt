Ithe Witũ Wĩ Igũrũ
(“Likania)

1.Ithe witũwĩ Igũrũ,
  Ũtũthikĩrĩrie;
  Twakũhoya na ngoro
  Ũikarage gwitũ.

2.Mwathani Jesũ Kristũ
  Mũhonokia witũ,
  Ũtũthambie ngoro
  Na thakame yaku.

3.Nawe Roho Mũtheru
  Mũteithũrani witũ,
  Ũtũteithie rĩu,
  Tũkũmenye wega.

4.Ithe, Mũriũ na Roho,
  Atatũ thĩinĩ wa ũmwe,
  Tũkwenda kũmũgooca,
  Ngai ũmwe wiki.
