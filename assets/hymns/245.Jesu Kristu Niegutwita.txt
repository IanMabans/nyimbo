Jesũ Kristũ Nĩegũtwĩta(189)
(Jesus Calls O’er the Tumult)

1.Jesũ Kristũ nĩegũtwĩta,
  Tũũmĩrĩrie mogwati;
  O mũthenya nĩoigaga,
  “ũkani mũ’hikĩre.”

2.Andũ a Galili tene
  Nĩmetirũo nĩ Jesũ,
  Moke mamũrũmĩrĩre
  Matuĩke arutwo ake.

3.Hĩndĩ ĩrĩa tũngĩgucio
  Nĩ mĩago ya gũkũ thĩ,
  Atwĩraga tũmwĩhoke
  Na tũmwende makĩria.

4.Maũndũinĩ maitũ mothe,
  Kwĩ mĩnoga na ikeno,
  We ndatigaga gũtũũria,
  “Nĩũnyendete makĩria?”

5.Mwathani Jesũ tũteithie
  Tũkwĩhokage kũna,
  Tũgũtungatĩre wega
  Twĩ na Wendo mũnene.

                Mat 4:18-22
